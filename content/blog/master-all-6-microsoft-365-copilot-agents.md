## What You'll Learn

Microsoft 365 Copilot now includes **six specialised AI agents** that go beyond the standard chat experience. Each agent is designed for a specific task — research, data analysis, meeting facilitation, and more. In this tutorial, you'll master all six:

- **Researcher Agent** — Automatically gathers, summarises, and organises information from the web and your M365 data
- **Analyst Agent** — Analyses your data in Excel and produces insights, trends, and recommendations
- **SharePoint Agent** — Creates a custom AI assistant grounded in your SharePoint site content
- **Facilitator Agent** — Helps run and summarise meetings in Teams with real-time notes and action items
- **Interpreter Agent** — Provides real-time voice translation in Teams meetings across languages
- **Project Manager Agent** — Helps track tasks, deadlines, and project status across Planner and Teams

> **Tip:** Each agent is accessible directly from the Copilot chat in Microsoft 365. Try them one by one in your own tenant.

---

## What Are Copilot Agents?

Copilot Agents are **specialised AI assistants** within Microsoft 365 that are pre-configured for specific tasks. Think of them as expert colleagues who each have a specific skill set:

| Agent | Best For | Where It Works |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| 🔬 **Researcher** | Deep research and briefing generation | BizChat, Word |
| 📊 **Analyst** | Data analysis, trends, and insights | Excel, BizChat |
| 📁 **SharePoint** | Q&A from your organisation's documents | SharePoint, Teams |
| 🎤 **Facilitator** | Meeting notes and action item tracking | Teams meetings |
| 🌍 **Interpreter** | Real-time voice translation | Teams meetings |
| 📋 **Project Manager** | Task and project tracking | Planner, Teams |

### How Are They Different from Regular Copilot?

Regular Copilot is a **general-purpose** assistant. Agents are **specialised** — they have pre-built prompts, specific data connections, and tailored outputs for their task. You don't need to engineer complex prompts; the agent knows what to do.

## Researcher Agent

The Researcher Agent automates the process of gathering information, organising it, and producing a structured briefing document.

### Example Use Cases

- "Research the latest trends in sustainable cloud computing and create a briefing"
- "Compile information about our top 3 competitors from public sources"
- "Summarise all internal documents about the Q2 product roadmap"

The output is a **Word document** with sections, citations, and key findings — ready to share with your team.

📖 [Microsoft 365 Copilot Agents documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-agents)

## Analyst Agent

The Analyst Agent works with your **Excel data** to find patterns, generate charts, and produce data-driven recommendations.

### Example Use Cases

- "Analyse our sales data and identify the top-performing regions"
- "Create a forecast for next quarter based on historical trends"
- "What anomalies do you see in our expense reports?"

📖 [Copilot in Excel documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-excel)

## SharePoint Agent

The SharePoint Agent creates a **custom AI assistant** grounded in the content of a specific SharePoint site. It can answer questions using only your organisation's documents.

### Example Use Cases

- Create an agent for your HR policy site — employees ask questions about leave, benefits, and policies
- Create an agent for your product documentation — the support team gets instant answers
- Create an agent for your onboarding site — new hires find information without asking colleagues

📖 [SharePoint agents overview](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/get-started-sharepoint-copilot)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are Copilot Agents included in the standard Copilot licence?**
Most agents are included with the Microsoft 365 Copilot licence ($30/user/month). Some may require additional configuration by your admin.

**Can I create my own custom agents?**
Yes — use **Copilot Studio** or the **SharePoint agent builder** to create agents grounded in your own data.

**Do agents have access to all my data?**
Agents follow the same security permissions as regular Copilot — they can only access data you already have permission to see.
