## What You'll Learn

This complete MS-900 course covers everything for the **Microsoft 365 Fundamentals** certification. You'll understand:

- **Cloud concepts** — Benefits of cloud services, SaaS vs on-premises, Microsoft 365 cloud architecture
- **Microsoft 365 apps and services** — Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, Exchange Online, Power Platform
- **Security and compliance** — Microsoft Entra ID, MFA, Conditional Access, Microsoft Purview, DLP
- **Microsoft 365 licensing** — E3 vs E5, Business vs Enterprise, add-on licences (Copilot, Intune)
- **Support and deployment** — Microsoft 365 admin centre, service health, adoption tools

> **Tip:** Perfect starting certification if you work in or support Microsoft 365 environments. No coding required.

## Key Topics Covered

### Cloud Concepts (10-15%)

- Why organisations move to the cloud — scalability, cost efficiency, automatic updates
- SaaS model for Microsoft 365 — you subscribe, Microsoft manages infrastructure
- Hybrid deployment options — some services on-premises, others in the cloud

### Microsoft 365 Apps and Services (45-50%)

This is the largest section:

- **Microsoft Teams** — Chat, video meetings, channels, Teams Phone, and collaboration
- **SharePoint Online** — Document management, team sites, intranet, and content collaboration
- **OneDrive for Business** — Personal cloud storage with sync and sharing
- **Exchange Online** — Enterprise email, calendar, and contacts
- **Microsoft 365 Apps** — Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook (desktop, web, and mobile)
- **Power Platform** — Power BI (analytics), Power Apps (custom apps), Power Automate (workflows), Power Virtual Agents (chatbots)
- **Viva** — Employee experience platform (Viva Engage, Viva Learning, Viva Insights)
- **Windows 365 and Azure Virtual Desktop** — Cloud-based desktops

### Security and Compliance (25-30%)

- **Microsoft Entra ID** — Identity management, SSO, MFA, and Conditional Access
- **Microsoft Defender for Office 365** — Email protection, safe links, safe attachments
- **Microsoft Purview** — Compliance solutions including DLP, retention policies, eDiscovery
- **Microsoft Intune** — Device management for Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android
- **Information protection** — Sensitivity labels and encryption

### Licensing and Support (10-15%)

- **Microsoft 365 plans** — Business Basic/Standard/Premium vs Enterprise E1/E3/E5
- **Add-on licences** — Microsoft 365 Copilot, Intune, Defender, Purview
- **Microsoft 365 admin centre** — User management, service health, and reporting
- **Support options** — Service requests, community forums, FastTrack deployment assistance

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is MS-900 worth it?**
Yes — especially if you work in a Microsoft 365 environment. It validates your understanding of the platform and is a prerequisite mindset for MS-102 (M365 Administrator).

**What's the difference between MS-900 and AZ-900?**
MS-900 covers **Microsoft 365** (Teams, SharePoint, Outlook, Intune). AZ-900 covers **Azure** (VMs, storage, networking, cloud concepts). They're complementary — many people get both.

**How hard is the MS-900?**
It's one of the easiest Microsoft exams. If you use Microsoft 365 at work, you already know much of the content.

**What comes after MS-900?**
The natural next steps are MS-102 (Microsoft 365 Administrator) or MS-700 (Teams Administrator).
