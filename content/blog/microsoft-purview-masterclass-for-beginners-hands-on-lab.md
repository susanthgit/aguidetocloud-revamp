## Key Topics Covered

Microsoft Purview is a unified data governance and compliance platform. It helps organisations protect sensitive information, manage data lifecycle, and meet regulatory requirements.

This hands-on lab covers information protection, sensitivity labels, data loss prevention (DLP), insider risk management, and audit capabilities.
