## What You'll Learn

This full PL-900 course covers the **Microsoft Power Platform Fundamentals** certification. You'll understand:

- **Power BI** — Create interactive dashboards, reports, and data visualisations from multiple data sources
- **Power Apps** — Build custom business applications without writing code (canvas and model-driven apps)
- **Power Automate** — Automate repetitive workflows between apps and services (cloud flows, desktop flows)
- **Power Virtual Agents / Copilot Studio** — Create AI-powered chatbots for customer and employee support
- **Dataverse** — The underlying data platform that stores and manages business data securely
- **AI Builder** — Add AI capabilities (form processing, object detection, text classification) to your apps and flows

## Key Topics Covered

### Power Platform Overview (15-20%)

- **What is Power Platform?** — A suite of low-code/no-code tools for building apps, automating processes, analysing data, and creating chatbots
- **Business value** — Citizen developers can build solutions without waiting for IT; faster time to value
- **Connectors** — 1,000+ pre-built connections to Microsoft and third-party services (SharePoint, SQL, Salesforce, Twitter, etc.)
- **Dataverse** — Cloud-based data store with built-in security, business logic, and integration

### Power BI (20-25%)

- **Data connections** — Import data from Excel, SQL, SharePoint, web APIs, and hundreds of other sources
- **Visualisations** — Bar charts, line charts, maps, cards, slicers, matrices, and custom visuals
- **DAX formulas** — Data Analysis Expressions for calculated columns, measures, and KPIs
- **Dashboards and reports** — Pin visualisations from multiple reports into a single dashboard
- **Sharing and collaboration** — Publish to web, share with colleagues, embed in Teams and SharePoint

### Power Apps (20-25%)

- **Canvas apps** — Drag-and-drop app builder with pixel-level control over layout
- **Model-driven apps** — Data-first apps built on top of Dataverse tables
- **Formulas** — Excel-like syntax for app logic (Filter, LookUp, Navigate, Patch)
- **Data sources** — Connect to Dataverse, SharePoint, SQL, Excel, and custom APIs

### Power Automate (20-25%)

- **Cloud flows** — Triggered by events (new email, form submission, file upload) or on a schedule
- **Desktop flows** — Automate legacy desktop applications with Robotic Process Automation (RPA)
- **Business process flows** — Guide users through multi-step processes with stages and conditions
- **Approvals** — Built-in approval workflows with Teams notifications

### Power Virtual Agents / Copilot Studio (10-15%)

- **Create chatbots** — Design conversational AI bots without coding
- **Topics and triggers** — Define what the bot responds to and how
- **Integration** — Connect bots to Power Automate flows for backend actions
- **Deploy** — Publish to Teams, websites, Facebook, and other channels

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is PL-900 easy?**
Yes — it's a fundamentals exam testing concepts and awareness. Most people pass with 1-2 weeks of study.

**Do I need coding skills for PL-900?**
No — Power Platform is a low-code/no-code platform, and the exam reflects that. No programming knowledge is required.

**Is Power Platform free?**
Power BI Desktop is free. Power Apps and Power Automate have free trials, but production use requires a licence (often included in Microsoft 365 E3/E5).

**What comes after PL-900?**
PL-200 (Power Platform Functional Consultant), PL-400 (Power Platform Developer), or PL-300 (Power BI Data Analyst).
