March 2026 brings **25 new updates** to Microsoft 365 Copilot, designed to make work smarter, faster, and more efficient.

A special shout-out to **Shelly Blackley**, whose hard work and persistence continue to power this monthly update series.

---

## 1. Edit with Copilot (Agent Mode) in Excel

Edit with Copilot (previously called 'Agent Mode') is now available for **Copilot Chat users** (without a paid licence) and on the **desktop app** (Windows and Mac, not just web).

If you have a paid licence, you may also select which model to use, including **both OpenAI and Anthropic models** (if your organisation allows it).

📖 [Agent Mode in Excel](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/agent-mode-in-excel-a2fd6fe4-97ac-416b-b89a-22f4d1357c7a)

## 2. Chat History Update

Click on 'Chats' in the left-hand menu to open your **Chat History landing page**. Updated filtering makes it simpler to find conversations and easier to clear filters.

## 3. Copilot with OneDrive Files in macOS Finder

Right-click on a file in Finder to **ask questions** about it using Microsoft 365 Copilot.

## 4. Scheduling with Copilot

Schedule meetings directly from chat. Ask Copilot to **find available times, book rooms, draft agendas, and send invites** — all within the chat experience.

## 5. Share with Summary for OneDrive Files

Click the Copilot icon in the share dialog to have Copilot **send a summary** of the file being shared. Available in File Explorer and OneDrive Activity Center.

## 6. Floating Copilot Button in OneDrive

A **quick-access button** appears prominently in your OneDrive web interface. It suggests helpful actions based on your file selection.

## 7. Loop Notebooks in Context IQ

Ground your prompts in Copilot Chat by scoping to **Loop Notebooks** content in the Files tab.

## 8. Review PDFs with Copilot

Copilot explains selected text in plain language and lets you **customise the prompt** for tailored results — no need to switch apps.

📖 [Review PDFs with Copilot](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/review-pdfs-using-copilot-context-menu-and-ai-actions-2ffbdafc-34e0-4019-8f3a-aceb57e9498b)

## 9. Agents in Classic Outlook

You can now access agents in Copilot Chat from **Classic Outlook** on Windows (previously New Outlook only). Type `@` or select the tools icon.

## 10. Audio Overview of Meetings

Copilot can generate an **audio overview** of your transcribed meetings — choose one or two speakers, adjust tone, and tailor the length. Currently English only.

📖 [Audio meeting recaps](https://support.microsoft.com/en-au/office/listen-to-audio-recaps-of-your-meetings-04bd2828-5c4e-4132-ac86-a87b9ec28984)

## 11. Ask Copilot from Edge Address Bar

Type a search query in the Edge address bar and send it to **Copilot** for a synthesised answer pulling from the web (and your work content for paid licence users).

## 12. Summarise & Explain Actions in PDF Reader

Microsoft Edge expands its PDF reader with two new actions — **'Summarise'** and **'Explain'** — to generate overviews or simplify complex content.

## 13. Copilot Can Edit Presentations in PowerPoint

Copilot now lets you **create, edit, and refine presentations** through natural conversation — directly in the presentation, not just the side panel. Connects to your **brand kit** for templates, images, and compliance.

## 14. Watermarks for AI-Generated Content

You can now add **watermarks** for AI-generated video, audio, and image content via the [Microsoft privacy dashboard](https://account.microsoft.com/privacy).

## 15. Enhanced Memory with Work Data

Copilot now uses **M365 Graph data** to provide more relevant, contextual responses. Manage what Copilot remembers in Settings > Personalisation.

## 16. Word Agent

Find the **Word Agent** under 'All agents' in the M365 Copilot app. It helps create Word files directly — handling research, structure, and formatting. Designed for long-form, information-dense work like strategic plans, policy documents, and technical papers.

Available to users **with or without** a paid licence (if admin allows).

## 17. SharePoint List Agent

Create lists effortlessly through prompts with **intelligent schema generation**. Transforms structured information and Copilot responses to Microsoft Lists.

## 18. Voice Chats Reference Memory

During voice chat, Copilot can respond using **existing memories** stored in personalisation settings. You can't modify memories via voice yet.

📖 [Copilot Voice](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/topic/using-copilot-voice-with-microsoft-copilot-efad42fc-d593-49c6-98bf-5ed94c881c32)

## 19. Voice Support in Word & PowerPoint

Talk and interact with Copilot verbally to **brainstorm ideas, create content, and analyse information**. Voice understands documents, work, and web context.

## 20. Metadata Support for SharePoint Queries

Copilot now leverages **column metadata** from selected SharePoint libraries, delivering more accurate results.

## 21. Overview Page in Copilot Notebooks

Copilot-powered **summaries and insights** as a landing page within your Notebook. Paid licence required.

## 22. Prepare for Meetings in Outlook Mobile

Outlook mobile users can **prepare for meetings** using Copilot Chat in a single tap. Paid licence required.

## 23. Copilot Chat for Teams Chats, Channels, Calling & Meetings

Copilot Chat will be available inside **Teams chats, channels, calling, and meetings**. You can ask questions about the meeting AND general Copilot questions like "Summarise the latest email about this project."

## 24. Copilot Chat Across Multiple Edge Tabs

Copilot Chat in Edge can answer contextual questions about **all your open tabs** — not just the active one.

📖 [Copilot in Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-au/topic/getting-started-with-copilot-in-microsoft-edge-ab0153dc-ad31-4de6-899a-802223821a9d)

## 25. Ground Copilot in YouTube Videos

Get relevant answers and insights about your **open YouTube videos** in Copilot Chat in Edge for Business.
