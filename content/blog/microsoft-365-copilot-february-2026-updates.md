As we move further into 2026, Microsoft 365 Copilot continues to evolve rapidly with **36 new updates** this month designed to make work smarter, faster, and more efficient.

A special shout-out to **Shelly Blackley**, whose hard work and persistence continue to power this monthly update series.

---

## 1. Agent Mode in Excel for Copilot Chat Users

Now available for Microsoft 365 subscribers **without** a paid M365 Copilot licence. Agent Mode enables multi-step workflows — create tables, charts, PivotTables, and formulas directly in your workbook.

📖 [Agent Mode in Excel](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/agent-mode-in-excel-a2fd6fe4-97ac-416b-b89a-22f4d1357c7a)

## 2. Copilot with OneDrive Files in macOS Finder

Interact with Copilot directly in Finder to ask questions about your OneDrive files. Enabled by default.

## 3. PowerPoint: Steer Presentation Length, Tone, Style & Images

You can now adjust the **length, tone, style, and AI-generated images** when creating presentations with Copilot.

## 4. Copilot Search: Source-Specific Filters

Dynamic filtering in Copilot Search lets you refine results by selecting data sources (Outlook, SharePoint, Jira, Copilot Chats) with **tailored filters** for each.

## 5. File Preview & Chat on Android

Opening Office files in the M365 Copilot app on Android now shows a **preview experience** with Copilot Chat for Q&A.

## 6. Auto-Summary on File Open in Word

When you open a document, Copilot **automatically generates a summary** in the Word window. You can hide it or open Copilot chat for follow-up questions.

## 7. Document Comments Summary in Word

Copilot adds a **Discussion tab** at the top of your document to summarise open comments, helping you quickly understand feedback.

## 8. Chat Can Modify Copilot Pages

When Chat and a Page are open side by side, prompts within Chat can now **modify or add** to the contents of the Page.

📖 [Copilot Pages documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-pages)

## 9. Ask Questions About Open Copilot Pages

You can now ask questions in chat about your open page, and Copilot provides answers **based on the page's contents**.

## 10. Auto-Generated Copilot Pages

Copilot Chat **automatically creates and opens** a Copilot Page side-by-side when it detects a relevant scenario, enabling instant editing and sharing.

## 11. PowerPoint from Copilot Pages

Use Copilot to generate a PowerPoint **grounded on a Copilot Page**. Reference it with `/` in your prompt or the paperclip icon.

## 12. Improved Python-Powered Answers in Excel

Copilot in Excel now provides improved direct answers using **Python** for data analysis.

## 13. Teams Channels in Context IQ

Search for and select **Teams Channels** in Context IQ to ground your prompts. Click the `+` and choose 'Channels'.

## 14. Summarise Email in Outlook (No Copilot Licence Required)

Users without a paid licence can now **summarise emails** in a single click using Copilot Chat in both Classic and New Outlook.

## 15. AI-Generated Agent Icons

Use AI to describe how your agent's icon should look — helping inform users of the agent's function. Enter custom prompts or use the agent description to generate the icon. Icons capped at 1 MB (192px).

## 16. Designer in Microsoft 365 Copilot

**Designer** is now part of M365 Copilot. Copilot Suggestions brings Designer slide layout and template suggestions in a vertical gallery.

## 17. Facilitator: Task Management & Document Creation in Meetings

**Facilitator** can help meeting participants manage tasks and create documents. Ask it to create, edit, and assign tasks (syncs to Planner), or draft documents based on meeting discussion.

📖 [Facilitator in Teams meetings](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/facilitator-in-microsoft-teams-meetings-37657f91-39b5-40eb-9421-45141e3ce9f6)

## 18. Copilot Actions in SharePoint Document Libraries

The Copilot button now appears when selecting items in **SharePoint document libraries** and Teams, offering contextual actions.

## 19. Agents in OneDrive

Build, use, and share your own agents using **files stored in OneDrive**. Requires a Microsoft 365 Copilot licence.

## 20. Copilot Chat Reasons Over Full Inbox & Calendar

Users without a paid licence can now use Copilot Chat in Outlook to reason over their **entire inbox and calendar**.

## 21. Edit Scheduled Prompts

You can now **edit your scheduled prompts** after creating them.

## 22. Scheduling with Copilot

Schedule meetings directly from chat. Ask Copilot to **find available times, book rooms, draft agendas, and send invites** — all within the chat experience.

## 23. Agents in Classic Outlook

Agents are now available in **Classic Outlook** for Windows, not just New Outlook. Use `@` mention to access agents.

## 24. Share with Summary for OneDrive Files

Summarise files and send the summary within the **share dialog** in File Explorer and OneDrive Activity Center.

## 25. Floating Copilot Icon in OneDrive File Previewer

A new Copilot button at the **bottom-right corner** of the file previewer surfaces contextual prompts and quick access to Copilot.

## 26. Voice Q&A During Read Aloud in Word

While your document is being read aloud, ask questions with your voice — "What does this mean?" or "Summarise this section" — and get **instant answers**. Currently en-US only.

## 27. Filter Search As You Type

Use **person and source filters** right in the Search box. Type "person" or "source" and relevant filter suggestions appear.

## 28. Search Matches on Department

Copilot Search can now match on a person's **department** and find all people in a given department.

## 29. Discover People in Search

Copilot Search returns matching people for broader search queries. Discover contacts through the **People data source** by skills like "Project leadership" or "Artificial Intelligence".

## 30. Agent Mode in Excel Desktop

Agent Mode now available on **Windows and Mac desktop** apps (previously web-only). Eligible licences can select which model to use, including **both OpenAI and Anthropic models**.

## 31. Scatter Image Effect

Apply **scatter image effect** to AI-generated or uploaded images in Copilot Chat — creating a sense of motion, depth, and energy.

## 32. Updated Copilot Notebooks Interface

Cleaner layout with **fixed navigation on the left** and chat on the right. Auto-generated overviews and suggested notebooks.

## 33. Quick Image Edit in PowerPoint

Edit images directly in PowerPoint with Copilot — **improve resolution, remove backgrounds**, and more without switching apps.

## 34. Meeting Recap Includes Screen-Shared Content

Intelligent meeting recap now incorporates **content shared on screen** in the AI summary, capturing unspoken insights.

📖 [Meeting recaps documentation](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/recap-in-microsoft-teams-c2e3a0fe-504f-4b2c-bf85-504938f110ef)

## 35. Calendar Search for Delegates

Delegates with calendar access can now **search for meetings** using Copilot Chat, saving time on scheduling tasks.

## 36. Memory & Personalisation Updates

Copilot uses chat history for more relevant responses. Updated settings for viewing and managing what Copilot remembers.
