## What You'll Learn

This full MS-700 course covers the **Microsoft Teams Administrator Associate** certification. You'll master:

- **Teams deployment and lifecycle** — Planning, deploying, and managing Teams across an organisation
- **Chat, collaboration, and channels** — Team creation policies, messaging policies, and guest access
- **Meetings and events** — Meeting policies, conference bridges, live events, and webinars
- **Teams Phone and voice** — Calling plans, Direct Routing, auto attendants, and call queues
- **Troubleshooting** — Call quality, network assessment, and Call Quality Dashboard (CQD)

> **Tip:** This is an associate-level certification. Having admin access to a Microsoft 365 tenant for hands-on practice is highly recommended.

## Key Topics Covered

### Plan and Configure Teams (35-40%)

- **Teams architecture** — How Teams integrates with SharePoint, OneDrive, Exchange, and Entra ID
- **Governance** — Naming policies, expiration policies, and Teams templates
- **Guest and external access** — Configuring who can be invited and what they can do
- **Teams admin centre** — The central management console for all Teams settings
- **Compliance** — Retention policies, communication compliance, DLP for Teams

### Manage Meetings and Calling (30-35%)

- **Meeting policies** — Who can schedule, recording permissions, lobby settings, and transcription
- **Audio conferencing** — Dial-in numbers, conference bridges, and communication credits
- **Teams Phone** — Calling plans vs Direct Routing vs Operator Connect
- **Auto attendants and call queues** — Automated call routing and IVR menus
- **Live events and webinars** — Large-scale broadcast meetings and registration-based events

### Troubleshoot and Monitor (25-30%)

- **Call Quality Dashboard (CQD)** — Analyse call and meeting quality across your organisation
- **Network assessment** — Testing network readiness for Teams voice and video
- **Real-time telemetry** — Monitor active calls and meetings
- **Teams advisor** — Automated assessment and deployment guidance
- **Log analysis** — Client logs, admin reports, and usage analytics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is MS-700 being retired?**
Check the [official exam page](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/m365-teams-administrator-associate/) for the latest status. Microsoft regularly updates certification paths.

**Do I need a real Teams environment to study?**
A Microsoft 365 trial tenant or [developer program](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/dev-program) is very helpful for hands-on practice.

**What's the difference between MS-700 and MS-900?**
MS-900 covers M365 **fundamentals** (concepts). MS-700 covers Teams **administration** (hands-on configuration and management).

### Certification Renewal

Microsoft role-based and specialty certifications **expire after one year**. To renew:

1. You'll receive an email 6 months before expiry
2. Complete a free online renewal assessment on Microsoft Learn
3. No exam centre visit required — it's done online
4. You can attempt the renewal assessment as many times as needed

> Fundamentals certifications (AZ-900, AI-900, SC-900, etc.) **do not expire** — they're valid for life.
