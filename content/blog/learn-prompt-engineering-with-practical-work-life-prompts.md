## Key Topics Covered

Prompt engineering is the skill of writing effective instructions for AI models. Whether you're using Microsoft 365 Copilot, ChatGPT, or any other AI tool — the quality of your prompt directly determines the quality of the output.

In this guide, you'll learn practical prompting techniques with real-world examples you can use immediately in your work and personal life.
