## What You'll Learn

This full MS-500 course covers the **Microsoft 365 Security Administrator** certification. You'll master:

- **Identity and access management** — Conditional Access, MFA, Identity Protection, and Privileged Identity Management
- **Threat protection** — Microsoft Defender for Office 365, Defender for Endpoint, Defender for Identity, and Defender for Cloud Apps
- **Information protection** — Sensitivity labels, DLP policies, Azure Information Protection, and encryption
- **Governance and compliance** — Retention policies, eDiscovery, audit logs, and insider risk management

## Key Topics Covered

### Identity and Access (25-30%)

- **Conditional Access policies** — Enforce MFA, block risky sign-ins, require compliant devices
- **Identity Protection** — Risk-based policies that detect suspicious sign-in behaviour
- **Privileged Identity Management (PIM)** — Just-in-time admin access with approval workflows
- **Authentication methods** — Passwordless (FIDO2, Windows Hello), OATH tokens, phone sign-in

### Threat Protection (30-35%)

- **Microsoft Defender for Office 365** — Safe Links, Safe Attachments, anti-phishing policies
- **Microsoft Defender for Endpoint** — Endpoint detection and response (EDR), attack surface reduction
- **Microsoft Defender for Identity** — On-premises Active Directory threat detection
- **Microsoft Defender for Cloud Apps** — Shadow IT discovery, app governance, session controls
- **Microsoft 365 Defender portal** — Unified incident management across all Defender products

### Information Protection and Governance (35-40%)

- **Sensitivity labels** — Classify and protect documents and emails with encryption and visual markings
- **Data Loss Prevention (DLP)** — Prevent accidental sharing of sensitive data (credit cards, health records, etc.)
- **Microsoft Purview** — Unified compliance and data governance
- **Retention policies** — Automatically retain or delete content based on age or conditions
- **eDiscovery** — Legal search, hold, and export for compliance investigations
- **Insider Risk Management** — Detect and act on risky behaviour by internal users

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is MS-500 being retired?**
Check the [official page](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/m365-security-administrator/) for the latest status. Microsoft has been consolidating security certifications.

**What's the difference between MS-500 and SC-900?**
SC-900 is fundamentals (concepts). MS-500 is associate-level (hands-on administration of M365 security features).

**How long to study for MS-500?**
4-6 weeks with daily hands-on practice in a Microsoft 365 tenant.

### Certification Renewal

Microsoft role-based and specialty certifications **expire after one year**. To renew:

1. You'll receive an email 6 months before expiry
2. Complete a free online renewal assessment on Microsoft Learn
3. No exam centre visit required — it's done online
4. You can attempt the renewal assessment as many times as needed

> Fundamentals certifications (AZ-900, AI-900, SC-900, etc.) **do not expire** — they're valid for life.
