This privacy policy explains what data **A Guide to Cloud & AI** (aguidetocloud.com) collects, why it's collected, who receives it, and your rights.

This is a personal educational website run by Susanth Sutheesh. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Microsoft.

## What We Collect

### Analytics (with your consent)

We use two analytics services that only activate **after you consent** via the cookie banner:

| Service | What It Collects | Purpose | Data Controller |
|---------|-----------------|---------|----------------|
| **Google Analytics 4** | Page views, device type, country, referral source, session duration | Understand which content is useful | Google LLC |
| **Microsoft Clarity** | Anonymised session recordings, heatmaps, click patterns | Improve page layouts and usability | Microsoft Corporation |

Both services use cookies and similar technologies. **Neither activates until you click "Accept" on the cookie banner.**

- **No personal information** (name, email, IP address) is collected by analytics
- Google Analytics uses anonymised, aggregated data only
- Microsoft Clarity does not capture keystrokes in text fields

### Feedback Form

If you submit the [feedback form](/feedback/), we collect:

| Field | Required? | Where It's Stored |
|-------|-----------|------------------|
| Category | Yes | Public GitHub Discussion |
| Subject | Yes | Public GitHub Discussion |
| Message | Yes | Public GitHub Discussion |
| Name | No | Public GitHub Discussion (if provided) |
| Email | No | **Not published** — stored only in server logs for reply purposes |
| Related Tool | No | Public GitHub Discussion |

⚠️ **Your feedback subject, message, and name (if provided) are published as a public [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/susanthgit/aguidetocloud-feedback/discussions).** Do not include sensitive personal information in your feedback. Email addresses are never published publicly.

### Browser Storage (Local Only)

Our tools use your browser's `localStorage` to remember your preferences (e.g., saved favourites, dark mode settings, tool progress). This data:

- **Never leaves your browser** — it is not sent to any server
- Can be cleared at any time via your browser settings
- Is not accessible by us or any third party

### Password Generator Breach Check

The optional "Check My Password" feature sends only the **first 5 characters of a SHA-1 hash** (not your password) to [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) using their k-anonymity API. Your actual password never leaves your browser.

## Cookies

| Cookie | Service | Type | Purpose |
|--------|---------|------|---------|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Analytics | Distinguish unique visitors |
| `_clck`, `_clsk`, `CLID` | Microsoft Clarity | Analytics | Session tracking and heatmaps |
| `agtc_consent` | This site | Functional | Remember your cookie consent choice |
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare | Necessary | Bot protection (set by hosting provider) |

**Analytics cookies are only set after you consent.** The consent and Cloudflare cookies are necessary for site operation.

## Your Rights

Under the **New Zealand Privacy Act 2020** and applicable international privacy laws, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request what personal information we hold about you
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your personal information
- **Withdraw consent** — revoke your analytics consent at any time via the cookie banner
- **Clear local data** — clear all browser-stored preferences by clearing your browser's localStorage

To exercise these rights, contact us via the [feedback form](/feedback/) or email (available on the [about page](/about/)).

### How to Opt Out of Analytics

1. **Click "Reject" on the cookie banner** — analytics will not load
2. **Revoke consent** — clear the `agtc_consent` cookie in your browser settings, then refresh
3. **Use a browser extension** — [Google Analytics Opt-Out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or any ad blocker
