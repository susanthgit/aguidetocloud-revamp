## Who Is Team Member For?

Team Member is for **employees who need to see ERP data but don't manage it** — managers approving purchase orders, staff entering timesheets, or executives viewing dashboards.

| Can Do ✅ | Can't Do ❌ |
|----------|------------|
| View reports and dashboards | Create customers/vendors |
| Approve workflows | Post GL entries |
| Enter timesheets and expenses | Manage inventory |
| Run basic reports | Configure the system |

> **💡 Cost saver:** Instead of buying full Essentials ($70) for everyone, give Team Member ($8) to view-only users and save $62/user/month.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. How many Team Member licences can I have?**

Unlimited. There's no ratio requirement between full and Team Member licences in Business Central.
