## Who Is D365 Finance For?

D365 Finance is for **mid-to-large enterprises** with complex financial operations — multi-entity, multi-currency, complex consolidation, and regulatory reporting needs.

**Key capabilities:**
- 💰 General ledger, chart of accounts, dimensions
- 📋 Accounts payable and receivable with automation
- 🏦 Cash and bank management, cash flow forecasting (AI)
- 📊 Financial reporting and consolidation
- 🌍 Multi-currency, multi-entity, intercompany transactions
- 📑 Budgeting, fixed assets, tax management
- 🤖 AI-powered cash flow predictions and late payment predictions

> **💡 Finance vs Business Central:** If you're an SMB (under ~250 users) with standard financials, Business Central Essentials ($70) is the better fit. D365 Finance is for enterprises with complex consolidation, regulatory needs, and multi-country operations.
