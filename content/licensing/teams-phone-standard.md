## What Is Teams Phone?

Teams Phone turns Microsoft Teams into a **full cloud phone system** — replacing your traditional PBX with call queues, auto attendants, voicemail, call transfer, and music on hold.

**Key capabilities:**
- 📞 Make/receive calls via Teams desktop, mobile, or IP phones
- 🔀 Call queues and auto attendants
- 📱 Voicemail with transcription
- 🔄 Call transfer, park, and forwarding
- 🎵 Music on hold, call recording

> **⚠️ Teams Phone ≠ PSTN.** Teams Phone gives you the software PBX. For actual phone numbers and PSTN connectivity, you need a **Calling Plan** ($8-15/user), **Operator Connect**, or **Direct Routing** (bring your own SBC).
