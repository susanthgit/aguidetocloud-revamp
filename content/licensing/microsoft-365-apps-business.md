## Who Is Apps for Business For?

This is a **niche plan** — desktop Office apps without any cloud services. It's for businesses that already have email and collaboration tools from another provider.

**Apps for Business is right for you if:**
- ✅ You already use **Google Workspace or another email provider**
- ✅ You just need **Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook desktop apps**
- ✅ You want **1 TB OneDrive** cloud storage per user
- ✅ Under 300 users

| Feature | Apps for Business ($10) | Business Standard ($14) |
|---------|:-----------------------:|:-----------------------:|
| Desktop Office Apps | ✅ | ✅ |
| OneDrive (1 TB) | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Exchange (email)** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Teams** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **SharePoint** | ❌ | ✅ |

> **💡 For $4 more,** Business Standard gives you email, Teams, and SharePoint. Unless you specifically DON'T want Microsoft cloud services, Standard is the better deal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Can I use Outlook without Exchange?**

Yes — the Outlook desktop app is included and can connect to any IMAP/POP email provider (Gmail, etc.). You just don't get Exchange Online.

**2. Is this the same as a standalone Office licence?**

Similar, but this is a subscription (monthly/annual). The benefit is always-current apps with cloud updates, plus 1 TB OneDrive.
