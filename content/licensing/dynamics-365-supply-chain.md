## Who Is SCM For?

D365 Supply Chain Management is for **manufacturing and distribution enterprises** with complex operations — multiple warehouses, production lines, and global supply chains.

**Key capabilities:**
- 📦 Procurement and vendor management
- 🏭 Manufacturing (discrete, process, lean)
- 🏢 Multi-site warehousing with advanced picking/put-away
- 🚚 Transportation management
- 📊 Demand forecasting and master planning (AI)
- ✅ Quality control and management
- 🔌 IoT-enabled asset management

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Can I use SCM without D365 Finance?**

Technically yes, but most enterprises use them together. Finance handles the money, SCM handles the goods. The combination is the core D365 ERP offering.
