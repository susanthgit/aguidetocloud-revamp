## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Can students use Education plans at home?**

Yes — A1 is free for students and includes web Office apps. A3/A5 can include student use benefits that allow desktop app installation on personal devices.

**2. Is Copilot available for Education?**

Microsoft 365 Copilot is available as an add-on for A3 and A5 plans, with education-specific pricing.
