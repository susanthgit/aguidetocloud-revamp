## Who Is Field Service For?

Field Service is for **organisations with mobile workers who visit customer sites** — HVAC technicians, electricians, maintenance crews, equipment installers.

**Key capabilities:**
- 📋 **Work order management** — create, assign, and track service jobs
- 📅 **Resource scheduling** — AI-optimised scheduling considering travel time, skills, and availability
- 📱 **Mobile worker app** — technicians get everything on their phone
- 🔌 **IoT integration** — connected devices auto-create work orders when issues detected
- 📦 **Inventory management** — track parts, truck stock, and warehouse levels
- 🤖 **Copilot** — automated scheduling suggestions and work order summaries

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Does Field Service work with Teams?**

Yes — technicians can video-call experts via Teams Assist directly from the mobile app for real-time guidance.
