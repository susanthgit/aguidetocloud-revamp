## Who Is Sales Professional For?

Sales Professional is the **entry-level D365 CRM** — basic lead and opportunity management for small teams that don't need AI forecasting or heavy customisation.

**Professional is right for you if:**
- ✅ You need basic **lead, opportunity, and quote management**
- ✅ Your sales process is **straightforward** without complex forecasting needs
- ✅ Budget is a concern — $30/user less than Enterprise

| Feature | Professional ($65) | Enterprise ($95) |
|---------|:------------------:|:----------------:|
| Leads & Opportunities | ✅ | ✅ |
| Quotes, Orders, Invoices | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Sales Forecasting (AI)** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Relationship Analytics** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **LinkedIn Integration** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Full Power Platform** | Limited | ✅ |

> **💡 Attach pricing:** If a user already has another D365 app, Sales Professional is available at a significant discount (~$20/user/month).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Can I upgrade from Professional to Enterprise later?**

Yes — seamless upgrade. Your data stays intact and you gain access to the advanced features immediately.
