## What's Included

- **Viva Connections** — personalised company dashboard in Teams with news, tasks, and resources
- **Viva Engage** — enterprise social network for communities, Q&A, and leadership AMAs
- **Viva Amplify** — create and distribute internal comms across Teams, email, and SharePoint simultaneously

> **💡 Best for:** Organisations focused on improving internal communications without needing the full analytics and learning modules.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is this cheaper than the Viva Suite?**

Yes — $2 vs $12/user. But you only get comms and communities, not analytics (Insights, Glint) or learning. If you want it all, the Suite is better value.
