## Who Is Office 365 E5 For?

O365 E5 is a niche plan — it adds **Teams Phone, Power BI Pro, and advanced compliance** over E3, but without the security suite (Intune, Defender, Entra P2) that Microsoft 365 E5 includes.

**O365 E5 is right for you if:**
- ✅ You need **Teams Phone System** without upgrading to Microsoft 365 E5
- ✅ You want **Power BI Pro** included for all users
- ✅ Security is handled by third-party tools

> **💡 For most organisations, M365 E5 at $60 is the better deal.** The extra $19 gets you the full security stack that would cost far more as individual add-ons.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is O365 E5 commonly used?**

Not as much as M365 E5. It's mainly used by organisations with existing security investments that just need the calling and analytics features.

**2. Can I add security features to O365 E5?**

Yes — you can add EMS E5 ($18/user) for Intune + Entra P2, but at that point ($41 + $18 = $59), you're basically paying the same as M365 E5 ($60).
