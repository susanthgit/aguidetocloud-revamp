## Who Is Sales Premium For?

Premium is for **enterprise sales teams that want full AI capabilities** — Copilot writes your follow-up emails, conversation intelligence analyses your sales calls, and predictive scoring tells you which deals will close.

**Premium adds over Enterprise ($95):**
- 🤖 **Copilot for Sales** — AI-drafted emails, meeting prep, deal summaries
- 📞 **Conversation Intelligence** — auto-records calls, analyses sentiment, suggests coaching
- 📊 **Predictive Scoring** — AI ranks leads and opportunities by likelihood to close

| Feature | Enterprise ($95) | Premium ($135) |
|---------|:----------------:|:--------------:|
| Full CRM + Forecasting | ✅ | ✅ |
| LinkedIn Integration | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Copilot for Sales** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Conversation Intelligence** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Predictive Scoring** | ❌ | ✅ |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is the $40 premium over Enterprise worth it?**

For sales teams making high-value deals where AI insights can influence close rates — yes. For transactional sales with simple processes, Enterprise is enough.
