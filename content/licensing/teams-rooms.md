## Basic vs Pro

| Feature | Basic (Free) | Pro ($40/room) |
|---------|:------------:|:--------------:|
| One-touch join | ✅ | ✅ |
| Max rooms | 25 | Unlimited |
| **Remote device management** | Basic | ✅ Advanced |

| **Pro Management portal** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Detailed analytics** | ❌ | ✅ |

> **💡 Under 25 rooms?** Basic is free and perfectly functional. Pro is for enterprise IT teams managing large meeting room estates across multiple offices.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is Teams Rooms a per-user or per-device licence?**

Per-room/device — not per-user. Each meeting room device (Poly, Logitech, Yealink, etc.) needs its own licence.
