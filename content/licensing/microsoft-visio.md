## Plan 1 vs Plan 2

| Feature | Plan 1 ($5) | Plan 2 ($15) |
|---------|:-----------:|:------------:|
| Visio for the web | ✅ | ✅ |
| Basic templates and shapes | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Visio Desktop app** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Data-linked diagrams** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Database reverse engineering** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Advanced templates** | ❌ | ✅ |

> **💡 Tip:** If you just need simple flowcharts, consider free alternatives (draw.io, Excalidraw) before buying Visio. Visio's value is in complex, data-linked enterprise diagrams.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is Visio included in any M365 plan?**

No — Visio is always a separate licence. However, basic Visio viewing is available in Teams and SharePoint for all M365 users.
