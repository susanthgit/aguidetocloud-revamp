## Professional vs Enterprise

| Feature | Professional ($45) | Enterprise ($250) |
|---------|:------------------:|:-----------------:|
| Full IDE | ✅ | ✅ |
| Debugging & profiling | ✅ | ✅ |
| Azure dev/test credits | $50/mo | **$150/mo** |
| **IntelliTest** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Load testing** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Architecture tools** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **CodeLens** | ❌ | ✅ |

> **💡 Most developers use VS Code** (free) for daily coding and only need Visual Studio for .NET enterprise development, C++ projects, or advanced debugging scenarios.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Can I use VS Community Edition instead?**

VS Community is free for individuals, open-source, and small teams (≤5 users in a company). Larger companies need Professional or Enterprise licences.
