## What's Included

- **Viva Insights** — workplace analytics, meeting effectiveness, focus time, manager dashboards
- **Viva Glint** — enterprise employee engagement surveys with industry benchmarks
- **Viva Pulse** — quick pulse surveys for managers to check in with teams

> **💡 Best for:** HR teams and leadership wanting data-driven people decisions — measuring engagement, identifying burnout, and tracking team health.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is personal Insights free?**

Yes — personal Viva Insights (your own focus time, meeting stats) is included in M365. This paid licence adds manager/leader analytics and org-wide Glint surveys.
