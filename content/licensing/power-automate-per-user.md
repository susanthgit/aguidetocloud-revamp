## Who Is Power Automate per User For?

Power Automate per User is for **anyone who automates workflows regularly** — approvals, data sync, notifications, document processing, and desktop automation.

**Per User is right for you if:**
- ✅ You automate workflows across **M365, D365, SharePoint, and third-party services**
- ✅ You use **premium connectors** (SAP, Salesforce, SQL Server)
- ✅ You do **attended desktop automation** (automating legacy apps on your PC)
- ✅ You need **Dataverse** for storing flow data

## Licensing Tiers

| Plan | Price | Key Feature |
|------|-------|------------|
| M365 included (basic) | Free | Standard connectors only, no Dataverse |
| **Per User** | $15/mo | Unlimited flows, premium connectors, attended RPA |
| **Per User + RPA** | $40/mo | + Unattended desktop automation |
| **Per Flow** | $500/mo | 5 flows shared across the org (no per-user cost) |

> **💡 The M365 trap (same as Power Apps):** Basic Power Automate is "included" in M365, but the moment you need a premium connector or Dataverse, you need the per User licence.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What's the difference between attended and unattended RPA?**

Attended RPA runs on your PC while you're logged in — like a macro on steroids. Unattended RPA runs on a virtual machine without anyone logged in — full automation.

**2. Can I share a flow with my team?**

Yes — but each user who runs a premium flow needs their own licence. The Per Flow plan ($500/month for 5 flows) is an alternative for org-wide flows.
