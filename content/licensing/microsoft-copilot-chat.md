## What Is Copilot Chat?

Copilot Chat is the **free AI chatbot** available to most M365 users. It can answer questions, generate text, and help brainstorm — but it does **NOT** access your organisation's data.

## Copilot Chat vs Microsoft 365 Copilot

| Feature | Copilot Chat (Free) | M365 Copilot ($30/mo) |
|---------|:-------------------:|:---------------------:|
| Price | Free | $30/user/month |
| AI chat interface | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Access your emails, files, calendar** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Works in Word, Excel, PowerPoint** | ❌ (removed April 2026) | ✅ |
| **Meeting summaries in Teams** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Grounded on enterprise data** | ❌ | ✅ |
| Available in standalone Copilot app | ✅ | ✅ |
| Available in Teams | ✅ | ✅ |

> **⚠️ April 2026 change:** Copilot Chat was removed from Word, Excel, and PowerPoint for users without the paid Copilot licence. This means free users can only access Copilot via the standalone app and Teams.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Where can I still use Copilot Chat for free?**

The standalone Copilot app (copilot.microsoft.com) and within Microsoft Teams. It's been removed from Word, Excel, and PowerPoint desktop/web apps.

**2. Should I pay for the full Copilot?**

If you live in Outlook and Teams, the meeting summaries and email drafting alone justify the $30. If you rarely use Office apps, the free Chat may be sufficient.
