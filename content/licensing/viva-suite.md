## What's in the Viva Suite?

| Module | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Viva Connections** | Company intranet dashboard in Teams |
| **Viva Engage** | Enterprise social network and communities |
| **Viva Amplify** | Multi-channel internal communications |
| **Viva Insights** | Workplace analytics, meeting effectiveness, focus time |
| **Viva Glint** | Employee surveys and sentiment analysis |
| **Viva Pulse** | Quick team check-ins and pulse surveys |
| **Viva Goals** | OKR tracking and goal alignment |
| **Viva Learning** | LMS integration and learning content |

## Suite vs Standalone Bundles

| Option | Price | Includes |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Viva Suite** | $12/user | All modules |
| Comms & Communities | $2/user | Connections, Engage, Amplify |
| Analytics & Feedback | $6/user | Insights, Glint, Pulse |
| Both standalone | $8/user | Missing Goals and Learning |

> **💡 The suite saves $4/user** if you want all modules — and you get Goals and Learning which aren't in the standalone bundles.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Do I need Viva for the basic Teams intranet?**

No — basic Viva Connections (intranet dashboard in Teams) is included with M365 plans that have SharePoint. The paid licence adds premium features.

**2. Can I mix Viva Suite and standalone licences?**

Yes — give the full suite to HR and leadership, standalone bundles to broader staff.
