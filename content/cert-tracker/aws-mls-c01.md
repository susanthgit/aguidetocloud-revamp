## About the MLS-C01 Exam

> Build, train, and deploy ML models on AWS

Master AWS machine learning — SageMaker, built-in algorithms, data engineering, feature engineering, model evaluation, MLOps, and AI services for end-to-end ML workflows.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The MLS-C01 is designed for **professionals specialising in a focused domain**. Prior certification or equivalent experience recommended.
