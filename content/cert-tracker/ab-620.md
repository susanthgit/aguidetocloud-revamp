## About the AB-620 Exam

> Build, extend, and integrate enterprise AI agents with Copilot Studio

The developer certification for building production-ready AI agents. MCP tools, A2A multi-agent, Foundry integration, computer use, enterprise knowledge sources, and ALM pipelines — with code examples and real integration patterns.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The AB-620 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
