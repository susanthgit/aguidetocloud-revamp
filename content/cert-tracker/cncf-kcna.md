## About the KCNA Exam

> Your gateway to cloud native — validate Kubernetes fundamentals

The CNCF Kubernetes and Cloud Native Associate (KCNA) certification validates foundational knowledge of Kubernetes, container orchestration, cloud native architecture, observability, and application delivery — designed for IT professionals entering the cloud native ecosystem.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The KCNA is designed for **beginners and career changers**. No prior experience required.

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study

Start with KCNA (Kubernetes & Cloud Native Associate) for fundamentals, then CKA (admin) or CKAD (developer), then CKS (security) or KCSA (security associate).
