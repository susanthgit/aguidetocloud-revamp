## About the DEA-C01 Exam

> Design and manage data pipelines on AWS

Master AWS data engineering — Glue, Kinesis, Redshift, Athena, EMR, S3, Lake Formation, and DMS for ingestion, transformation, storage, operations, and governance.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DEA-C01 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
