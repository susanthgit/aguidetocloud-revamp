## About the ML-ENGINEER Exam

> Build, deploy, and manage ML models and AI solutions on Google Cloud.

The ML/AI specialist Google Cloud certification. Updated October 2024 with generative AI focus. Covers BigQuery ML, Vertex AI, custom training, MLOps pipelines, model monitoring, responsible AI, and foundation models.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The ML-ENGINEER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
