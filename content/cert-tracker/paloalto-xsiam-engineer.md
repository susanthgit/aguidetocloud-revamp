The XSIAM-Engineer is designed for **security operations, detection, and platform engineers** who deploy, integrate, and operate Cortex XSIAM in a SOC. It validates the hands-on skills to stand up a tenant, onboard endpoint/network/cloud/identity data, build automation playbooks, tune detection content, and troubleshoot the data pipeline and components.

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks alongside hands-on Cortex XSIAM experience
