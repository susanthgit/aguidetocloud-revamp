## About the WORKSPACE-ADMIN Exam

> Manage and secure Google Workspace for your organisation.

The Google Workspace admin certification. Covers organisational management, user lifecycle, service configuration (Gmail, Drive, Meet), security policies, DLP, SSO, mobile device management, and compliance.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The WORKSPACE-ADMIN is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
