## Who Should Take This Exam?

The JNCIA-MistAI is designed for **network and wireless engineers, NOC and helpdesk staff, and IT administrators working with — or moving into — Juniper Mist cloud-managed networking**. Introductory wireless-networking knowledge helps but is not required, and there is no prerequisite certification.

**Typical study time:** 3-5 weeks of focused study

## Juniper Certification Path

Juniper follows: Associate (JNCIA) → Specialist (JNCIS) → Professional (JNCIP) → Expert (JNCIE). On the Mist AI track, JNCIA-MistAI (JN0-253) is the associate entry point, followed by JNCIS-MistAI-Wireless (JN0-451) and JNCIS-MistAI-Wired (JN0-452), then JNCIP-MistAI (JN0-653).
