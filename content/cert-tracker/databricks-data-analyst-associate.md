## About the Databricks Certified Data Analyst Associate Exam

> Master the Databricks Certified Data Analyst Associate exam (Oct 2025 guide) — analyzing data with Databricks SQL and SQL Warehouses, the Databricks Assistant, joins, aggregations and set operations, managed and external tables, materialized views and Delta Lake time travel, query optimization with Photon, query history and Liquid Clustering, AI/BI Dashboards and visualizations, AI/BI Genie spaces, dimensional data modeling, and securing data with Unity Catalog.

The complete practice exam for the Databricks Certified Data Analyst Associate certification (current Oct 30, 2025 exam guide). Covers the Databricks Data Intelligence Platform and managing data (core platform components — Mosaic AI, Delta Live Tables, Lakeflow Jobs, Delta Lake, Unity Catalog and Databricks SQL; Catalog Explorer with catalogs, schemas, managed and external tables, views, certified tables, lineage and tagging; Databricks Marketplace; discovering, querying and cleaning certified datasets; and importing data via S3 ingestion, Delta Sharing, API-driven intake, Auto Loader, Marketplace and Workspace UI upload), executing queries with Databricks SQL and SQL Warehouses (the Databricks Assistant for writing and debugging queries, the role of a SQL Warehouse, aggregate operations, sorting and filtering, joins and set operations, cross-system analytics with Lakehouse Federation, materialized views versus streaming tables, managed and external tables, and Delta Lake time travel), analyzing and optimizing queries (Photon, Query Insights and the Query Profiler, Delta Lake history and auditing, query history and caching, Liquid Clustering, and fixing queries), building dashboards and visualizations (AI/BI Dashboards with multi-page layouts and widgets, notebook and SQL editor visualizations, query and dashboard parameters, sharing and permissions, scheduled refresh, SQL Alerts, and choosing the right visualization type), developing, sharing and maintaining AI/BI Genie spaces (purpose and components, sample questions and instructions, Trusted Assets, permissions and distribution, and optimization), and data modeling and securing data (star, snowflake and data vault schemas, the Medallion Architecture, Unity Catalog roles and the three-level namespace, table ownership and PII protection) — every question a real-world scenario with full explanations.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The Databricks Certified Data Analyst Associate is designed for **data analysts, BI developers, and analytics engineers** who use the Databricks Data Intelligence Platform to query data, build reports, and deliver insights. It validates practical skills across Databricks SQL and SQL Warehouses, Unity Catalog data discovery and management, query analysis and optimization, AI/BI Dashboards and visualizations, AI/BI Genie natural-language analytics, dimensional data modeling, and securing data. Databricks recommends related course attendance and roughly six months of hands-on experience performing the data analysis tasks in the exam guide.

**Prerequisites:** None (related training and ~6 months of hands-on Data Analyst experience recommended)

**Typical study time:** 3-6 weeks of focused study
