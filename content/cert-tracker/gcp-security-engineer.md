## About the SECURITY-ENGINEER Exam

> Secure Google Cloud environments with IAM, encryption, network controls, and compliance.

The security-focused Google Cloud professional certification. Covers IAM, network security, data protection, encryption, DLP, Security Command Center, incident response, and regulatory compliance.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The SECURITY-ENGINEER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
