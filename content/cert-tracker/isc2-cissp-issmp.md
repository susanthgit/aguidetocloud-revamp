## About the CISSP-ISSMP Exam

> Security programme leadership — the CISO concentration

The (ISC)² CISSP-ISSMP concentration validates expertise in leading and managing enterprise security programmes, covering security leadership, systems lifecycle management, risk management, threat intelligence, contingency management, and security compliance — the most managerial of all (ISC)² certifications.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CISSP-ISSMP is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
