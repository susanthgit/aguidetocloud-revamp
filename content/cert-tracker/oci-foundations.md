## About the OCI-FOUNDATIONS Exam

> Master the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) 2026 Foundations Associate exam (1Z0-1085-26) — OCI distributed cloud, regions and availability domains, the core Compute, Networking, and Storage services, the shared security model with IAM and OCI security services, and OCI pricing, cost management, and support.

The complete practice exam for the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2026 Foundations Associate certification (exam 1Z0-1085-26, current June 2026 version — replaces the retired 1Z0-1085-25). A conceptual, entry-level certification that proves you can explain OCI's core services and value. Covers Getting Started with OCI (the OCI distributed cloud, Public Cloud, Dedicated Region and Cloud@Customer, regions, availability domains and fault domains, high availability and disaster recovery concepts), Core OCI Services (Compute instances and flexible/bare-metal/preemptible shapes, autoscaling, container instances, OKE and Functions; Virtual Cloud Networks, subnets, gateways, security lists and NSGs, Load Balancer and DNS, FastConnect and VPN; Object Storage tiers and lifecycle, Block Volume performance tiers and backups, File Storage, and Archive Storage), Security Services (the OCI shared-responsibility model, Cloud Guard, Security Zones, Vault and KMS, Web Application Firewall, Bastion and Vulnerability Scanning, plus Identity and Access Management with identity domains, policies, compartments, and dynamic groups), and Governance and Administration (the OCI pricing model with pay-as-you-go and Annual Universal Credits, Always Free resources, BYOL, the cost estimator, Budgets, Cost Analysis, Usage Reports, quotas, tagging, and OCI support and SLAs) — every question a real-world scenario with full explanations, so you understand the why, not just the what.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The OCI 2026 Foundations Associate (1Z0-1085-26) is designed for **anyone starting with Oracle Cloud Infrastructure** — career changers, students, and sales, technical, or IT staff adding OCI to their cloud skills. The exam is entirely conceptual, so **no hands-on experience is required**, and it is the recommended first step before the OCI Architect Associate and the Developer, Security, Networking, and Generative AI tracks.

**Prerequisites:** None (no hands-on experience required; a recommended starting point for OCI certifications)

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study
