## About the JNCIS-MistAI-Wired Exam

> Specialist Juniper Mist Wired Assurance - switch deployment, operations, Campus Fabric, and EVPN-VXLAN

250 original practice questions for the Juniper Networks Certified Specialist - Mist AI Wired
(JNCIS-MistAI-Wired, exam JN0-460). The bank focuses on intermediate wired decisions: provisioning and templating
supported switches, applying port profiles, diagnosing Wired SLEs, selecting a Campus Fabric architecture, and
reasoning about the EVPN-VXLAN control and data planes.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The JNCIS-MistAI-Wired is designed for **wired LAN engineers, campus network engineers, Mist implementation
consultants, network architects, and operations staff** who already understand the Mist platform and need deeper
skills in switch deployment, wired assurance, and standards-based campus fabrics.

**Prerequisite:** JNCIA-MistAI.

**Typical study time:** 5-8 weeks of focused study, depending on hands-on EX/QFX, Mist, and EVPN-VXLAN experience.

## Juniper Mist AI Certification Path

The current Mist AI path starts with **JNCIA-MistAI (JN0-253)**. You can then take either
**JNCIS-MistAI-Wireless (JN0-452)** or **JNCIS-MistAI-Wired (JN0-460)**. Either specialist certification satisfies
the stated prerequisite path into **JNCIP-MistAI (JN0-750)**.

## What to Study First

1. **Build the Wired Assurance object map** - organization, site, switch, template, site variable, port profile, wired client, SLE, and insight.
2. **Separate static and dynamic port operations** - know what a port profile defines, what DPC matches, and when a restricted fallback profile helps.
3. **Learn the three Campus Fabric choices** - EVPN multihoming, Core-Distribution, and IP Clos, including where CRB and ERB place the gateway.
4. **Master the EVPN-VXLAN planes** - Layer-3 underlay, MP-BGP EVPN control plane, VXLAN data plane, VTEP, VNI, and MAC learning.
5. **Know the route-type jobs** - Type 1 A-D, Type 2 MAC/IP, Type 3 IMET, Type 4 Ethernet Segment, and Type 5 IP Prefix.
6. **Use Juniper terminology** - Mist portal, SLE, IRB, LAG/ae, ESI-LAG, GBP, CRB, and ERB.
