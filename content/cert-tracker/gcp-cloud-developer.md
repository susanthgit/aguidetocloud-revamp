## About the CLOUD-DEVELOPER Exam

> Build, test, and deploy scalable applications on Google Cloud.

The developer-focused Google Cloud professional certification. Covers cloud-native application design, CI/CD pipelines, deploying to GKE/Cloud Run/App Engine, API management, and integrating GCP services.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CLOUD-DEVELOPER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
