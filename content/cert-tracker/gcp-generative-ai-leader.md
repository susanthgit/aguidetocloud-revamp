## About the Generative AI Leader Exam

> Lead your organization's generative AI strategy on Google Cloud — Gemini, Vertex AI, RAG, and responsible AI, no coding required.

The Google Cloud Generative AI Leader is a foundational, business-oriented certification for the people who decide how an organization adopts generative AI. Instead of testing code or configuration, it checks that you understand generative AI concepts, Google Cloud's AI offerings, the techniques that make model output reliable, and the business strategy and responsible-AI practices that turn a pilot into real value.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

This certification is built for **business leaders, managers, product owners, strategists, and decision-makers** — anyone who needs to understand how generative AI can transform their organization without needing a deep technical background. No coding or hands-on engineering experience is required; a working, business-level understanding of generative AI is all you need to start.

**Typical study time:** 2-3 weeks of part-time study

The Generative AI Leader exam covers **4 domains**. Focus your study time based on the weights below — higher-weighted domains have more exam questions.

## Study Tips

1. **Know the model families cold** — Gemini (multimodal reasoning), Gemma (open, lightweight), Imagen (images), and Veo (video). Mixing these up is the most common mistake.
2. **Understand the "why," not the "how"** — this is a leadership exam. You don't need to configure anything; you need to know when to use grounding, RAG, prompt engineering, or fine-tuning.
3. **Use our practice exam** — try the 20 free questions first to gauge your readiness.
4. **Learn the responsible-AI vocabulary** — fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, and Google's Secure AI Framework (SAIF) show up across the exam.
5. **Check the official page** — [official exam details](https://cloud.google.com/learn/certification/generative-ai-leader) always have the latest objectives.
