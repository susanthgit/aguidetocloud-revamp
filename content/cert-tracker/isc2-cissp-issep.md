## About the CISSP-ISSEP Exam

> Engineer security into systems from requirements to disposal

The (ISC)² CISSP-ISSEP concentration validates expertise in applying systems engineering principles to build secure systems throughout their lifecycle, with emphasis on NIST RMF, verification and validation, and formal engineering processes.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CISSP-ISSEP is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
