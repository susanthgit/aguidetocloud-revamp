## About the DEVOPS-ENGINEER Exam

> Build CI/CD pipelines and apply SRE practices on Google Cloud.

The DevOps and SRE certification for Google Cloud. Covers CI/CD pipelines, SLIs/SLOs/error budgets, incident management, monitoring strategies, infrastructure as code, and performance optimisation.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DEVOPS-ENGINEER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
