## About the CISSP Exam

> The gold standard in information security management

The (ISC)² Certified Information Systems Security Professional certification validates expertise in designing, implementing, and managing a best-in-class cybersecurity programme across eight domains of security knowledge.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CISSP is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Prerequisites:** 5 years security experience (or 4 + degree)

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study

## ISC² Certification Path

Start with CC (Certified in Cybersecurity) for entry-level, then SSCP for technical security, then CISSP for management. CISSP concentrations (ISSAP, ISSEP, ISSMP) come after CISSP.
