## About the DEVASC Exam

> Network automation with Python, APIs, and Cisco platforms

Master network programmability — REST APIs, Python scripting, Cisco platform SDKs (Meraki, DNA Center, ACI), Docker, CI/CD, NETCONF/RESTCONF, Ansible, YANG models, and network fundamentals. Original practice questions. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sourced from Cisco Systems certification exams.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DEVASC is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
