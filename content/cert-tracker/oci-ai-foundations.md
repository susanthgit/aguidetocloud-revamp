## About the OCI-AI-FOUNDATIONS Exam

> Master the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) 2026 AI Foundations Associate exam (1Z0-1122-26) — the fundamentals of artificial intelligence, machine learning and deep learning, generative AI and large language models, and the OCI AI portfolio including OCI AI Services and the OCI Generative AI service with Oracle Database 23ai AI Vector Search.

The complete practice exam for the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2026 AI Foundations Associate certification (exam 1Z0-1122-26, the current 2026 version that replaces the retired 1Z0-1122-25). A conceptual, entry-level certification that proves you can explain core AI concepts and the OCI AI portfolio — no coding or prior AI experience required. Covers AI Foundations (what AI is, narrow vs general AI, AI use cases by industry, the relationship between AI, ML and DL, structured vs unstructured and labeled vs unlabeled data, and common AI tasks), Machine Learning Foundations (supervised learning with regression and classification, model evaluation metrics like accuracy, precision, recall, F1, RMSE and R-squared, overfitting and underfitting and the bias-variance tradeoff, unsupervised learning with clustering and dimensionality reduction, reinforcement learning, and feature engineering), Deep Learning Foundations (artificial neural networks, activation functions, backpropagation and gradient descent, convolutional neural networks for images, and recurrent and LSTM networks for sequences), Generative AI and Large Language Models (generative vs discriminative AI, tokenization, embeddings and the context window, the Transformer architecture and self-attention, prompt engineering with zero-shot, few-shot and chain-of-thought, inference parameters like temperature and top-p, Retrieval-Augmented Generation and vector search, fine-tuning and PEFT/LoRA, and responsible AI and bias), the OCI AI Portfolio (OCI Language, Vision, Speech, Document Understanding, Anomaly Detection and Forecasting pretrained services, plus OCI Data Science, the model catalog, ML Jobs, AI Quick Actions, and OCI GPU AI infrastructure), and the OCI Generative AI Service and Oracle 23ai (the managed OCI Generative AI service with hosted Cohere and Meta Llama models, dedicated AI clusters, T-Few fine-tuning, OCI Generative AI Agents, AI Vector Search in Oracle Database 23ai, and Oracle Select AI) — every question a real-world scenario with full explanations, so you understand the why, not just the what.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The OCI 2026 AI Foundations Associate (1Z0-1122-26) is designed for **anyone who wants to understand AI, machine learning, and generative AI** and how Oracle Cloud Infrastructure delivers them — developers, data and business analysts, product owners, and sales, marketing, or IT staff adding AI to their cloud skills. The exam is entirely conceptual, so **no coding or prior AI experience is required**, making it a strong first step before the OCI Generative AI Professional and OCI Data Science certifications.

**Prerequisites:** None (no prior AI/ML familiarity or coding experience required)

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study
