## About the DATABASE-ENGINEER Exam

> Design, deploy, migrate, and manage databases on Google Cloud.

The database specialist Google Cloud certification. Covers database selection, high availability, migration strategies, Cloud SQL, Spanner, AlloyDB, Bigtable, Firestore, and performance tuning.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DATABASE-ENGINEER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
