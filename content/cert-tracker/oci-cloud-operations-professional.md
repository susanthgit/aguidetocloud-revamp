## About the OCI-CLOUD-OPERATIONS-PROFESSIONAL Exam

> Master the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2026 Cloud Operations Professional exam (1Z0-1067-26) - running OCI environments day to day: deploying with Resource Manager and the OCI CLI, configuring with Ansible and cloud-init, optimizing cost with Budgets, Compartment Quotas, Cloud Advisor and Resource Scheduler, ensuring reliability with instance-pool autoscaling and Full Stack Disaster Recovery, managing identity and security with IAM policies, Vault and dynamic groups, and implementing observability with alarms, logging, OS Management Hub and troubleshooting.

The complete practice exam for the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2026 Cloud Operations Professional certification (exam 1Z0-1067-26, validated against Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2026). A professional-level certification for cloud operations engineers, site-reliability engineers, cloud administrators, platform engineers, and DevOps engineers who operate and manage OCI environments day to day. Covers Deploying and Managing Resources (manual vs replicable deployment, the OCI CLI, and Infrastructure as Code with OCI Resource Manager - the managed Terraform service, its stacks and jobs, configuration source providers and private endpoints), Utilizing Configuration Management Tools (agentless Ansible with the OCI Ansible Collection and dynamic inventory, cloud-init user data and cloud-config, custom images and instance configurations, and configuration drift), Optimizing Cost and Performance (Budgets and budget alerts, Compartment Quotas and Service Limits, Cost Analysis and Usage Reports, Cloud Advisor recommendations, Resource Scheduler auto stop and start, right-sizing, and Operations Insights capacity planning), Implementing Reliability and Business Continuity (instance-pool autoscaling, load balancing, Full Stack Disaster Recovery with protection groups and switchover, failover and drill plans, and backup and data-retention strategies), Managing Identity and Security (IAM policy verbs and compartments, Identity Domains, dynamic groups and resource principals, OCI Vault master encryption keys and secrets, and least-privilege access control), and Implementing Observability (enabling metrics, alarms and health checks, log data patterns and visualizations, OS Management Hub and Fleet Application Management patching, and troubleshooting and diagnostics across OCI services) - every question a real-world operational scenario with full explanations, hints, exam tips, and why-wrong rationales.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2026 Cloud Operations Professional (1Z0-1067-26) is designed for **cloud operations engineers, site-reliability engineers, cloud administrators, platform engineers, and DevOps engineers** who operate and manage OCI environments day to day — deploying and destroying resources with the OCI CLI and Resource Manager IaC, configuring instances with Ansible and cloud-init, optimizing spend with Budgets, Compartment Quotas, Cloud Advisor and Resource Scheduler, ensuring reliability with instance-pool autoscaling and Full Stack Disaster Recovery, managing identity and security with IAM policies, OCI Vault and dynamic groups, and implementing observability with alarms, logging, OS Management Hub patching and cross-service troubleshooting. It validates professional-level, hands-on operations skills, so **practical experience running and managing OCI resources is strongly recommended**.

**Prerequisites:** None required (Oracle recommends hands-on experience deploying, configuring, and operating OCI resources)

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
