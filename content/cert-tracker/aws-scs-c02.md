## About the SCS-C02 Exam

> Secure AWS workloads, data, and infrastructure

Master AWS cloud security — GuardDuty threat detection, CloudTrail logging, KMS encryption, IAM policies, VPC architecture, WAF protection, incident response automation, and multi-account governance for the Security Specialty exam.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The SCS-C02 is designed for **professionals specialising in a focused domain**. Prior certification or equivalent experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks of focused study
