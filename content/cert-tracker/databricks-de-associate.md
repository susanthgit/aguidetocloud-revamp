## Who Should Take This Exam?

The Databricks Certified Data Engineer Associate is designed for **data engineers and data professionals** who build and operate data pipelines on the Databricks Data Intelligence Platform. It validates practical, entry-level data-engineering skills across ingestion, transformation, orchestration with Lakeflow Jobs, CI/CD, and Unity Catalog governance.

**Prerequisites:** None (course attendance + 6 months hands-on Databricks experience recommended)

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study

## Exam Domains & Weights

The DATABRICKS-DE-ASSOCIATE exam covers **7 domains**. Focus your study time based on the weights below — higher-weighted domains have more exam questions.

| Domain | Weight | Practice Qs |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Databricks Intelligence Platform | 6% | 15 |
| Data Ingestion and Loading | 21% | 53 |
| Data Transformation and Modeling | 22% | 55 |
| Working with Lakeflow Jobs | 16% | 40 |
| Implementing CI/CD | 10% | 25 |
| Troubleshooting, Monitoring, and Optimization | 10% | 25 |
| Governance and Security | 15% | 37 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **250** |

> 💡 **Study tip:** Data Transformation and Modeling (22%) and Data Ingestion and Loading (21%) together make up well over half the exam — get fluent with PySpark and Spark SQL transformations, Auto Loader vs COPY INTO, and the bronze/silver/gold medallion pattern. Governance and Security (15%) rewards hands-on Unity Catalog practice (managed vs external tables, GRANT/REVOKE, row filters and column masks). The smaller CI/CD and Troubleshooting domains (10% each) lean on Databricks Asset Bundles and reading the Spark UI for skew and spill.
