## About the AB-210 Exam

> Configure AI-enhanced Dynamics 365 Sales — the four sales agents, Copilot, predictive scoring, and the lead-to-cash pipeline (Microsoft's beta cert AB-210).

AB-210 is a **beta exam** (schedulable now via Pearson VUE at a reduced fee) that **replaces the retiring MB-280**. It validates your ability to design and configure AI-enhanced sales solutions across the lead-to-cash process.

Master Exam AB-210 (Accelerating Sales Pipelines with AI in Dynamics 365) — configure Dynamics 365 Sales core features for AI (business process flows, the Sales security model, the product catalog, mailboxes, and Microsoft 365 integration), optimize AI-driven sales (Sales accelerator, work assignment, conversational intelligence, predictive lead and opportunity scoring, relationship intelligence, forecasting, and goals), qualify and prioritize leads with the Sales Qualification Agent, develop deals with the Sales Opportunity Agent, Sales Close Agent, and Sales Research Agent, and extend Sales with the mobile app, Teams calling, an SMS channel, and Power Platform (Power Automate, embedded Power Apps and Power BI).

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The AB-210 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
