## About the GH-900 Exam

> Your first step into GitHub. Learn Git, repositories, collaboration, Actions, Copilot, and security — no prior experience needed.

The complete beginner's guide to GitHub. Covers version control and Git fundamentals, repositories and key files, collaboration with issues and pull requests, modern development with GitHub Actions, Copilot, and Codespaces, project management, privacy and security, administration, and the open-source community — all explained in plain English with real-world scenarios.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The GH-900 is designed for **beginners and career changers**. No prior experience required.

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study
