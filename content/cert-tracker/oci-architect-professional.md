## Who Should Take This Exam?

The OCI 2026 Architect Professional (1Z0-997-26) is designed for **senior cloud architects, solution architects, and infrastructure leads** who design, deploy, and operate enterprise-grade solutions on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure — spanning high availability and disaster recovery, cloud-native and serverless application platforms, Infrastructure as Code, database design, security architecture, hybrid and multicloud connectivity, migration, and observability. It validates advanced, hands-on architecture skills, so **practical experience designing OCI solutions across compute, storage, networking, databases, and security is strongly recommended**. It builds directly on the OCI Architect Associate certification.

**Prerequisites:** None (OCI Architect Associate-level knowledge plus hands-on experience architecting OCI solutions recommended)

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks of focused study
