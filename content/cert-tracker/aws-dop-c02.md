## About the DOP-C02 Exam

> Automate deployments, operations, and infrastructure on AWS

Master AWS DevOps — CI/CD with CodePipeline, CloudFormation IaC, monitoring with CloudWatch and X-Ray, incident automation, security compliance, and disaster recovery across multi-Region architectures.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DOP-C02 is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
