## About the SC-401 Exam

> Microsoft Purview — sensitivity labels, DLP, retention, insider risk, Adaptive Protection, and DSPM for AI.

The Microsoft Purview information security cert (replaces SC-400). Sensitivity labels, DLP, retention, insider risk, Adaptive Protection, and DSPM for AI.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The SC-401 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
