## About the CLOUD-ENGINEER Exam

> Deploy, manage, and secure applications and infrastructure on Google Cloud.

The hands-on associate-level Google Cloud certification. Covers deploying applications, configuring networks, managing compute and storage, monitoring operations, and securing access.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CLOUD-ENGINEER is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
