## About the CAPA Exam

> Automate delivery and workflows on Kubernetes with the Argo Project

Master the Argo Project ecosystem — Argo Workflows (the Workflow spec, template types, WorkflowTemplates and CronWorkflows, DAG dependencies, artifacts, and data-processing jobs), Argo CD (declarative GitOps continuous delivery, the Application CRD, automated sync with prune and self-heal, sync waves and hooks, Helm and Kustomize sources, and reconciliation), Argo Rollouts (the Rollout CRD, blue-green and canary progressive delivery, traffic management, and AnalysisTemplate/AnalysisRun), and Argo Events (event-driven automation with EventSources, the EventBus, Sensors, and Triggers). Original practice questions covering all 4 official CAPA domains. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sourced from CNCF or Linux Foundation certification exams.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CAPA is designed for **platform engineers, DevOps engineers, SREs, and data engineers** who build delivery pipelines and event-driven automation with the Argo Project on Kubernetes. There are no formal prerequisites; hands-on experience with Kubernetes and at least one Argo tool (Workflows, CD, Rollouts, or Events) is recommended.

**Typical study time:** 3-5 weeks of focused study
