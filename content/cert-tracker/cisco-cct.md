## About the CCT Exam

> Hands-on Cisco hardware installation and troubleshooting

Master Cisco field technician skills — hardware identification, cabling and connectors, IOS basics, boot process, LED troubleshooting, rack installation, PoE, TAC support cases, RMA process, and warranty management. Original practice questions. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sourced from Cisco Systems certification exams.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CCT is designed for **beginners and career changers**. No prior experience required.

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study

The CCT exam covers **4 domains**. Focus your study time based on the weights below — higher-weighted domains have more exam questions.
