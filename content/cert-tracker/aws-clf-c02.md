## About the CLF-C02 Exam

> Foundational AWS cloud knowledge for everyone

Master AWS fundamentals — cloud concepts, security and compliance, core services (EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, VPC), billing and pricing models, and support plans. Original practice questions. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sourced from Amazon Web Services certification exams.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CLF-C02 is designed for **beginners and career changers**. No prior experience required.

**Prerequisites:** None — 6 months cloud experience recommended

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study
