## About the DAS-C01 Exam

> Design and build analytics solutions on AWS

Master AWS data analytics services — Kinesis, Glue, Redshift, Athena, EMR, QuickSight, and Lake Formation for collection, storage, processing, analysis, and security.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DAS-C01 is designed for **professionals specialising in a focused domain**. Prior certification or equivalent experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks of focused study
