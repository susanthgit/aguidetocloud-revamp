## Who Should Take This Exam?

The JNCIA-Cloud is designed for **networking and IT professionals moving into cloud, SDN/NFV, and automation**. Introductory hands-on exposure to Linux, containers, or any cloud platform is helpful but not required.

**Typical study time:** 3-6 weeks of focused study
