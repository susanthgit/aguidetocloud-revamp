## About the AB-731 Exam

> Lead AI strategy, Copilot deployment, and adoption — the certification for C-suite and business decision-makers.

The new certification for business leaders driving AI transformation. AI strategy, Copilot capabilities, Foundry tools, and adoption planning — no coding required.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The AB-731 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
