## About the SAP-C02 Exam

> Design complex, multi-account, enterprise-scale AWS architectures

Master AWS architecture at scale — Transit Gateway, Organizations, multi-Region DR, migration strategies, serverless modernization, cost optimization, and security controls for enterprise workloads spanning hundreds of accounts.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The SAP-C02 is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
