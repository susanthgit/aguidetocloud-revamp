## About the SnowPro Advanced: Data Analyst Exam

> Master the Snowflake SnowPro Advanced: Data Analyst certification (DAA-C01) — data ingestion and preparation, transformation and modeling, advanced data analysis, and data presentation and visualization in Snowflake.

The complete practice exam for the Snowflake SnowPro Advanced: Data Analyst certification (DAA-C01). This advanced, role-based exam goes deeper than SnowPro Core — it tests how you ingest, transform, analyze, and present data on the Snowflake AI Data Cloud. Covers data ingestion and preparation (loading structured and semi-structured CSV/JSON/Parquet/Avro/ORC/XML data, COPY INTO and stages, ASOF and set-operation joins, Snowflake-maintained statistics, Marketplace and Secure Data Sharing enrichment, external tables, and pipeline automation with Snowpipe, streams, tasks, and dynamic tables), data transformation and data modeling (LATERAL FLATTEN and semi-structured parsing, Data Metric Functions, window and analytic functions with QUALIFY, CTEs and recursive queries, sampling and approximation, Time Travel and zero-copy cloning, views vs materialized views vs dynamic tables, and query optimization with clustering, caching, Search Optimization, and Query Acceleration), data analysis (UDFs and UDTFs, synchronous and asynchronous stored procedures, secure and materialized views, Snowsight dashboards and Notebooks, statistics and trends, and forecasting with built-in ML functions and Cortex Analyst), and data presentation and visualization (Snowsight charts and dashboards, custom filters, Streamlit in Snowflake, row access and masking effects on reports, BI tool connectivity, subscriptions and operationalized reporting) — every question a real-world analyst scenario with full explanations.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The SnowPro Advanced: Data Analyst certification is designed for **data analysts, BI developers, and analytics engineers** who explore, transform, and present data on the Snowflake AI Data Cloud. It validates advanced, hands-on skill across the analytics lifecycle: ingesting and shaping data, modeling and optimizing queries, running diagnostic and forecasting analyses, and building dashboards and reports. Candidates typically have 2+ years of hands-on Snowflake and SQL experience.

**Prerequisites:** SnowPro Core Certified

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks of focused study (plus hands-on Snowsight, SQL, and dashboard experience)

## Exam Domains & Weights

The SnowPro Advanced: Data Analyst exam covers **4 domains**. Focus your study time based on the weights below — higher-weighted domains have more exam questions.

| Domain | Weight | Practice Qs |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Data Analysis | 32% | 80 |
| Data Presentation and Data Visualization | 28% | 70 |
| Data Transformation and Data Modeling | 23% | 57 |
| Data Ingestion and Data Preparation | 17% | 43 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **250** |

> 💡 **Study tip:** Data Analysis (32%) is the heaviest domain — know UDFs and UDTFs, synchronous and asynchronous stored procedures, secure and materialized views, Snowsight dashboards and Notebooks, statistics and trend analysis, and forecasting with built-in ML functions and Cortex Analyst. Data Presentation and Visualization (28%) is Snowsight charts and dashboards, custom filters, Streamlit, BI connectivity, and how row access and masking policies affect reports. Data Transformation and Data Modeling (23%) covers window functions and QUALIFY, FLATTEN, CTEs, sampling, dynamic tables, and query optimization. Data Ingestion and Data Preparation (17%) is COPY INTO and stages, joins and set operations, Marketplace enrichment, and pipeline automation.

## Study Tips

1. **Lead with analysis** — the heaviest domain rewards knowing UDF/UDTF, stored procedures, views, Snowsight dashboards and Notebooks, statistics, and forecasting cold
