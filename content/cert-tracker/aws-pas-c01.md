## About the PAS-C01 Exam

> Design, migrate, and operate SAP workloads on AWS

Master SAP on AWS — HANA deployment, system replication, migration strategies (DMO/SWPM), HA/DR clustering, Backint backups, cost optimisation, and operations for mission-critical SAP landscapes.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The PAS-C01 is designed for **professionals specialising in a focused domain**. Prior certification or equivalent experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks of focused study
