## About the SAA-C03 Exam

> Design secure, resilient, high-performing cloud architectures

Master AWS architecture — VPCs, EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, CloudFront, IAM, and Well-Architected Framework for designing scalable, fault-tolerant cloud solutions.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The SAA-C03 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Prerequisites:** 1+ year hands-on AWS experience recommended

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
