## About the DBS-C01 Exam

> Design, deploy, and manage database solutions on AWS

Master Amazon RDS, Aurora, DynamoDB, and purpose-built databases. Migration strategies, performance tuning, security, and cost optimization for the AWS DBS-C01 specialty exam.
