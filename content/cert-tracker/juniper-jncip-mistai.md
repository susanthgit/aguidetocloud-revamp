operations.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The JNCIP-MistAI is designed for **network automation engineers, Mist platform engineers, network architects,
security engineers, and senior operations staff** who already understand the Mist portfolio and need to automate,
secure, and troubleshoot it across campus, branch, WAN, access, routing, and data-center environments.

**Prerequisite:** either JNCIS-MistAI-Wireless or JNCIS-MistAI-Wired.

**Typical study time:** 6-10 weeks of focused study, depending on hands-on Mist and Python/API experience.

## Juniper Mist AI Certification Path

The current Mist AI track progresses from **JNCIA-MistAI (JN0-253)** to either
**JNCIS-MistAI-Wireless (JN0-452)** or **JNCIS-MistAI-Wired (JN0-460)**, then to
**JNCIP-MistAI (JN0-750)**. The professional exam accepts either specialist certification as its prerequisite.

## What to Study First

1. **Map the Assurance portfolio** - know the boundaries between Wireless/Wi-Fi, Wired, WAN, Routing, Access, and Data Center Assurance.
2. **Automate safely** - practise short Python workflows, regional REST endpoints, scopes, tokens, pagination, batching, retries, Webhooks, and WebSockets.
3. **Separate the Marvis surfaces** - Actions, Minis, Conversational Assistant, Query Language, Client, and the Data Center assistant are not interchangeable.
4. **Understand the security actors** - supplicant, authenticator, RADIUS/Access Assurance, MAB, authorization outcomes, WxLAN, and SSR policy.
5. **Think in operational phases** - Day 1 creates dependencies and deploys; Day 2 collects evidence, remediates safely, and verifies the result.
