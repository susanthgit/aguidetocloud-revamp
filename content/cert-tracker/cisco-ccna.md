## About the CCNA Exam

> The world's #1 networking certification

Master networking fundamentals — routing, switching, VLANs, OSPF, subnetting, wireless, security, and automation. Original practice questions covering all 6 CCNA domains. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sourced from Cisco Systems certification exams.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CCNA is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study

## Exam Domains & Weights

The CCNA exam covers **6 domains**. Focus your study time based on the weights below — higher-weighted domains have more exam questions.

| Domain | Weight | Practice Qs |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Network Fundamentals | 20% | 40 |
| Network Access | 20% | 40 |
| IP Connectivity | 25% | 50 |
| IP Services | 10% | 20 |
| Security Fundamentals | 15% | 30 |
| Automation and Programmability | 10% | 20 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **200** |

> 💡 **Study tip:** **IP Connectivity** carries the most weight (25%) — start there. **IP Services** has the least (10%), but don't skip it — exam questions can come from any domain.
