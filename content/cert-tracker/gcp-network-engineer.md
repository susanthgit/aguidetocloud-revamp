## About the NETWORK-ENGINEER Exam

> Design, implement, and manage network architectures on Google Cloud.

The networking specialist Google Cloud certification. Covers VPC design, load balancing, hybrid connectivity (VPN, Interconnect), DNS, Cloud Armor, firewall policies, and network monitoring.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The NETWORK-ENGINEER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
