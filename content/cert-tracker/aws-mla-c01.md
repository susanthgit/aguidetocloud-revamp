## About the MLA-C01 Exam

> Build, deploy, and maintain ML solutions on AWS

Master AWS ML engineering — SageMaker Pipelines, Feature Store, Model Monitor, Clarify, and deployment patterns for building production ML systems on AWS.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The MLA-C01 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
