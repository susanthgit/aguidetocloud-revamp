## About the Associate Data Practitioner Exam

> Ingest, store, analyze, pipeline, and govern data on Google Cloud - BigQuery, Dataflow, Looker, Cloud Storage, and IAM, at associate depth.

The Google Cloud Associate Data Practitioner is an entry-level certification for the people who work with data on Google Cloud day to day. It checks that you can pick the right service for a job - where to store data, how to load and transform it, how to analyze and present it, and how to keep it governed and secure - across Google Cloud's data stack. It is practical and service-focused: the exam is about choosing the right tool and knowing why, not about writing production pipelines from scratch.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

This certification is built for **data analysts, junior data engineers, BI developers, and data practitioners** who are early in their Google Cloud journey. Google recommends around **6 months of hands-on experience** working with data on Google Cloud, plus a basic understanding of cloud service models. No deep software-engineering background is required - if you can write SQL and reason about which service fits a requirement, you are ready to start.

**Typical study time:** 3-5 weeks of part-time study

## Exam Domains & Weights

The Associate Data Practitioner exam covers **4 domains**. Focus your study time based on the weights below - higher-weighted domains have more exam questions.

| Domain | Weight | Practice Qs |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Data Preparation and Ingestion | 30% | 75 |
| Data Analysis and Presentation | 27% | 68 |
| Data Pipeline Orchestration | 18% | 45 |
| Data Management | 25% | 62 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **250** |

> 💡 **Study tip:** **Data Preparation and Ingestion** carries the most weight (30%) - be fluent in choosing a storage service (Cloud Storage vs BigQuery vs Cloud SQL vs Bigtable vs Spanner vs Firestore) and an ingestion tool (Storage Transfer Service, Transfer Appliance, BigQuery Data Transfer Service, Database Migration Service, Dataflow). **Data Management** (25%) is close behind: master IAM least-privilege roles, Cloud Storage lifecycle and classes, high availability, and the CMEK / CSEK / GMEK encryption-key choices.

## GCP Certification Path

The Associate Data Practitioner sits at the **associate** tier, a natural first data certification. From here, Google's data path runs up to the **Professional Data Engineer** (designing and building data-processing systems) and the **Professional Cloud Database Engineer** (managing Google Cloud databases) for deeper, hands-on technical roles.

## Study Tips

1. **Learn service selection cold** - most questions come down to "which service fits this requirement?" Know the sweet spot for Cloud Storage, BigQuery, Cloud SQL, Bigtable, Spanner, and Firestore, and for Dataflow vs Dataproc vs Cloud Data Fusion vs Dataform.
2. **Master the governance details** - IAM basic vs predefined roles, Cloud Storage lifecycle rules and storage classes, and the difference between GMEK, CMEK (Cloud KMS), and CSEK encryption keys.
3. **Know Looker vs Looker Studio** - Looker is the governed, LookML-modeled enterprise platform; Looker Studio is the free, self-serve reporting tool. Pick the right one for the audience.
4. **Use our practice exam** - try the 20 free questions first to gauge your readiness, then work domain by domain.
5. **Check the official page** - [official exam details](https://cloud.google.com/learn/certification/data-practitioner) always have the latest objectives.
