## About the CCSP Exam

> Cloud security architecture and governance

The (ISC)² Certified Cloud Security Professional certification validates advanced competence in cloud security architecture, design, operations, and service orchestration across major cloud platforms.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The CCSP is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study

## ISC² Certification Path

Start with CC (Certified in Cybersecurity) for entry-level, then SSCP for technical security, then CISSP for management. CISSP concentrations (ISSAP, ISSEP, ISSMP) come after CISSP.
