## Who is this exam for?

This Microsoft Security certification covers security, compliance, and identity topics. It tests your ability to implement and manage security solutions across Microsoft's cloud platforms. This is an associate-level exam that expects hands-on experience. You should have practical knowledge of the technologies covered.
