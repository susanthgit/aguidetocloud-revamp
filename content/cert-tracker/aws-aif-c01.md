## About the AIF-C01 Exam

> Foundational AI, ML, and generative AI knowledge on AWS

Demonstrate foundational knowledge of AI/ML concepts, generative AI, foundation models, prompt engineering, RAG, responsible AI, and security for AI solutions on AWS — covering Amazon Bedrock, SageMaker, and AWS AI services.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The AIF-C01 is designed for **beginners and career changers**. No prior experience required.

**Typical study time:** 2-4 weeks of part-time study
