> Implement advanced Cisco wireless — FlexConnect, QoS, multicast, location services, client security, monitoring, and device hardening

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The WLSI is designed for **experienced professionals validating advanced wireless implementation skills**. It is a **concentration exam of the new standalone CCNP Wireless track** (pair it with the 350-101 WLCOR core exam to earn CCNP Wireless) and it independently earns the **Cisco Certified Specialist – Wireless Advanced Solutions** credential. 3-5 years of enterprise networking experience with a wireless focus is recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
