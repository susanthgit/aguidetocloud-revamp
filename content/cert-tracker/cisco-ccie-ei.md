  - question: "What's the CCIE EI lab format exactly?"

All three must pass independently — you can ace Deploy but fail because Troubleshoot didn't reach the minimum score. The lab uses real Cisco IOS and IOS-XE devices in a virtualised environment, no GUI shortcuts. Bring water and snacks; you can't leave the room except for short breaks."
