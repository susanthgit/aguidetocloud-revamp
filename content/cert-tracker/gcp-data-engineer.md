## About the DATA-ENGINEER Exam

> Design, build, and optimise data processing systems on Google Cloud.

Google Cloud's premier data certification. Covers data pipeline design, ingestion, storage selection, analytics, BigQuery, Dataflow, data governance with Dataplex, and ML-ready data preparation.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DATA-ENGINEER is designed for **experienced professionals seeking advanced validation**. 2+ years of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 8-12 weeks of intensive study
