## Who Should Take This Exam?

The JNCIS-SEC is designed for **security and network engineers who deploy and operate Juniper SRX firewalls**. JNCIA-SEC (the Security Associate) is the recommended prerequisite, and hands-on SRX configuration experience helps a lot.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study

## Juniper Certification Path

Juniper follows: Associate (JNCIA) → Specialist (JNCIS) → Professional (JNCIP) → Expert (JNCIE). On the Security track that is JNCIA-SEC → JNCIS-SEC → JNCIP-SEC → JNCIE-SEC. JNCIS-SEC is the specialist step, best taken after JNCIA-SEC.
