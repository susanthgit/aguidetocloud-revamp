## About the DVA-C02 Exam

> Build and deploy cloud-native applications on AWS

Master AWS development — Lambda, DynamoDB, API Gateway, S3, Cognito, KMS, CodePipeline, SAM, CDK, X-Ray, and CloudWatch for building, securing, deploying, and troubleshooting cloud-native applications.

## Who Should Take This Exam?

The DVA-C02 is designed for **IT professionals with some hands-on experience**. 6-12 months of hands-on experience recommended.

**Typical study time:** 4-8 weeks of focused study
