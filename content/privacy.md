This privacy policy explains what data **A Guide to Cloud & AI** (aguidetocloud.com) collects, why, who receives it, and your rights.

This is a personal educational website run by Susanth Sutheesh. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Microsoft.

## What We Collect

### Analytics (with your consent)

We use two analytics services that only activate **after you consent** via the cookie banner:

| Service | What It Collects | Purpose |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| **Google Analytics 4** | Page views, device type, country, referral source, session duration | Understand which content is useful |
| **Microsoft Clarity** | Anonymised session recordings, heatmaps, click patterns | Improve page layouts and usability |

Both use cookies. **Neither activates until you click "Accept".**

- No personal information (name, email, IP) is collected by analytics
- Google Analytics uses anonymised, aggregated data only
- Microsoft Clarity does not capture keystrokes in text fields

### Feedback Form

If you submit the [feedback form](/feedback/), we collect:

| Field | Required? | Where It Goes |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| Category, Subject, Message | Yes | Public GitHub Discussion |
| Name | No | Public GitHub Discussion (if provided) |
| Email | No | **Never published** — for reply purposes only |

⚠️ Feedback subject, message, and name (if provided) are published as public [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/susanthgit/aguidetocloud-feedback/discussions). Do not include sensitive information. **Emails are never published.**

### Browser Storage (Local Only)

Our tools use `localStorage` for preferences (favourites, progress, settings). This data **never leaves your browser** and can be cleared anytime via browser settings.

### Password Generator Breach Check

The optional breach check sends only the **first 5 characters of a SHA-1 hash** (not your password) to [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) using k-anonymity. Your actual password never leaves your browser.

## Cookies

| Cookie | Service | Type | Purpose |
|--------|---------|------|---------|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Analytics | Distinguish unique visitors |
| `_clck`, `_clsk`, `CLID` | Microsoft Clarity | Analytics | Session tracking |
| `agtc_consent` | This site | Functional | Remember your consent choice |
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare | Necessary | Bot protection |

**Analytics cookies are only set after you consent.** Consent and Cloudflare cookies are necessary for site operation.

## Your Rights

Under the **New Zealand Privacy Act 2020** and applicable international privacy laws:

- **Access** — request what personal information we hold about you
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your personal information
- **Withdraw consent** — clear the `agtc_consent` item from localStorage and refresh the page
- **Clear local data** — clear browser localStorage via browser settings

### How to Opt Out of Analytics

1. **Click "Reject"** on the cookie banner — analytics will not load
2. **Revoke consent** — clear `agtc_consent` from localStorage, then refresh
3. **Use a browser extension** — [Google Analytics Opt-Out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or any ad blocker
