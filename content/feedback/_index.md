faq:
  - question: "How do I submit feedback?"
    answer: "Fill out the form on the page — pick a category (question, feature request, bug report, etc.), give it a title, and write your message. Hit Submit and it's posted instantly as a GitHub Discussion. No login or sign-up required."
  - question: "Will I get a response to my feedback?"
    answer: "Yes. Every submission is read and responded to directly in the GitHub Discussion thread. You can bookmark the link to check for replies, or browse all community discussions on the page."
  - question: "Can I request a new tool or suggest a video topic?"
    answer: "Absolutely. Choose 'Feature Request' or 'Video Request' from the category dropdown and describe what you'd like. Popular requests are prioritised and many tools on this site started as community suggestions."
  - question: "Is my feedback public?"
    answer: "Yes. Submissions are posted as public GitHub Discussions so the whole community can upvote and discuss ideas. No personal data beyond what you type in the form is collected — there's no login, email, or tracking."
  - question: "Is this tool still being improved?"
    answer: "Yes! This is a V1 release and we're actively improving it based on user feedback. If you have suggestions, find a bug, or want a new feature, please visit our Community Feedback page at aguidetocloud.com/feedback/ — every piece of feedback is read and acted on."
