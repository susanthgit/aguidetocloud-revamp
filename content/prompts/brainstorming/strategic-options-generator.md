## Tips for Best Results

- Replace [DECISION/INITIATIVE] with specifics: "whether to build or buy our CRM"
- Great for executive decision memos — follow up with "Write this as a decision paper"
- Add "Our risk appetite is [conservative/moderate/aggressive]" for tailored options
- Works well as input for board papers or steering committee meetings
