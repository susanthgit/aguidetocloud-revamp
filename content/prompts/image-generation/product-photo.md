## Tips for Best Results

- Replace `[PRODUCT]` with a specific description (e.g., "a matte black wireless headphone" not just "headphones")
- **Midjourney** produces the most photorealistic product shots — use `--v 6` for best quality
- In **DALL-E**, be very specific about lighting and camera angle
- Add material descriptions: "brushed aluminium", "frosted glass", "matte silicone"
