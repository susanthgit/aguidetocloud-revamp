## Tips for Best Results

- In **Outlook**, select the entire thread and ask Copilot to summarise
- In **Copilot Chat (Work mode)**, say "Summarise the email thread about [topic]"
- Essential for catching up on threads with 20+ replies
- Follow up with "Draft my reply addressing the open action items"
