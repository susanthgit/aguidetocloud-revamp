## Tips for Best Results

- Replace [ISSUE] and [RECIPIENT/ROLE] with specifics
- The key is being factual and solution-oriented — never accusatory
- Add "Include timeline of events" for complex escalations
- Follow up with "Draft a resolution summary email once this is fixed"
