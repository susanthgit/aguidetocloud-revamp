## Tips for Best Results

- Paste your original email draft directly below this prompt
- In **M365 Copilot (Outlook)**, highlight your draft and use "Rewrite with Copilot" — then paste just the instruction part
- In **ChatGPT/Claude**, paste the full prompt followed by your email text
- Add context like "This is for my VP of Engineering" to get better tone matching

## Variations

### Apologetic Tone
> Rewrite this email with a sincere, apologetic tone. Acknowledge the issue, take responsibility, and clearly outline next steps. Keep it professional and under 150 words.

### Friendly but Firm
> Rewrite this email in a friendly but firm tone. Be warm and approachable while clearly setting expectations and deadlines. Keep it under 150 words.
