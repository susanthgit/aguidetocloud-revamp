## Tips for Best Results

- Replace `[TECH A]` and `[TECH B]` with specific products (e.g., "Azure vs AWS" or "Notion vs Confluence")
- **Perplexity** excels here because it searches the web for current pricing and features
- In **Claude/ChatGPT**, add "As of 2026" to get the most current knowledge
- Add "Include a section on migration complexity" if you're switching tools

## Variations

### Three-Way Comparison
> Compare [TECH A], [TECH B], and [TECH C] in a feature matrix table. Score each 1-5 on: cost, ease of use, enterprise features, community support, documentation quality. Include a total score and recommendation.

### For Non-Technical Stakeholders
> Compare [TECH A] and [TECH B] for a non-technical audience. Skip jargon. Focus on business impact: cost savings, time savings, risk reduction, and user experience. Include a "Bottom Line" recommendation in one sentence.
