## Tips for Best Results

- In **M365 Copilot (Excel)**, select your data range first, then use Copilot — it reads the spreadsheet directly
- In **ChatGPT**, paste data as a table or upload a CSV file
- In **Claude**, you can paste large datasets — it handles long context well
- Be specific about the time period: "monthly over the last 12 months" gives better results than just "over time"
