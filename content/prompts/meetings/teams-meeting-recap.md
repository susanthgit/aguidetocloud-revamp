## Tips for Best Results

- Use in **Teams** immediately after a meeting — Copilot has the transcript
- Also works from **Copilot Chat (Work mode)**: "Recap my last meeting"
- Perfect for **EAs** who need to distribute meeting minutes
- Follow up with "Send this recap to all attendees"
