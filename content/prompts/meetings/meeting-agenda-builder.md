## Tips for Best Results

- Replace `[DURATION]`, `[MEETING TYPE]`, and `[TOPIC]` with your specifics
- In **M365 Copilot (Outlook)**, use when scheduling a meeting to auto-generate the agenda in the invite body
- Common meeting types: "sprint planning", "quarterly review", "1:1", "project kickoff", "budget review"
- Add "Include an icebreaker question for the first 2 minutes" for team-building meetings

## Variations

### Recurring Team Meeting
> Create a recurring weekly team meeting template (30 min). Sections: Quick Wins (5 min), Blockers (10 min), Priority Alignment (10 min), Open Floor (5 min). Include a rotating facilitator schedule.

### Workshop Format
> Create a 2-hour workshop agenda for [TOPIC]. Include interactive exercises, breakout sessions, and a collaborative output. Balance presentation time (30%) with hands-on activities (70%).
