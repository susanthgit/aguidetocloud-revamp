## Tips for Best Results

- Use **Microsoft Forms** to send the survey, then analyse results with Copilot
- In **Teams**, create a Loop component for the team to give live feedback
- Run monthly or after major changes (reorg, project completion, etc.)
- Follow up with "Draft a message to the team sharing the pulse results and our action plan"
