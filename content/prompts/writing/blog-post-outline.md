## Tips for Best Results

- Replace `[TOPIC]` and `[AUDIENCE]` with your specific details
- In **Claude**, this works exceptionally well because of its structured thinking
- Follow up with "Now write the full blog post based on this outline" for a complete draft
- Ask for "Include internal linking opportunities" if you have an existing blog

## Variations

### Listicle Format
> Create a listicle blog outline: "X [TOPIC] Tips Every [AUDIENCE] Should Know". Make it scannable with numbered H2s, each with a one-line summary and 2-3 detail bullets.

### How-To Guide
> Create a step-by-step how-to guide outline for [TOPIC]. Number each step as an H2. Include prerequisites, time estimate, and a "Common Mistakes" section at the end.
