## Tips for Best Results

- Dump your raw notes, bullet points, or even voice transcription below the prompt
- In **M365 Copilot (Work mode)**, it can pull context from your emails and calendar for the week
- In **Loop**, great for collaborative status updates across a team
- Add "Include a 'Shoutouts' section for team recognition" for team morale

## Variations

### Project Status
> Create a project status report: (1) RAG Status (Red/Amber/Green) with justification, (2) Milestone progress with dates, (3) Budget status, (4) Risk register update, (5) Decisions needed from leadership.

### Team Summary
> Compile a team weekly summary from these individual updates. Group by project, highlight cross-team dependencies, and call out any conflicting priorities or resource constraints.
