prompt: >-
  Facilitate a retrospective for our team covering [SPRINT/PERIOD]. Structure: (1) Collect input in three columns — What went well, What could improve, What to try next, (2) Group similar feedback into themes, (3) Let the team vote on the most important items (each person picks their top 3), (4) For the top-voted items, define a specific action, owner, and success measure, (5) Compare with last retro actions — did we follow through? Keep the tone constructive and forward-looking.

## Tips for Best Results

- Everyone types simultaneously — faster and more honest than speaking in turns
- Claude summarising themes prevents the retro from becoming a complaint session
- Track actions from previous retros to build accountability
