## Tips for Best Results

- Replace [CHANGE/INITIATIVE] with specifics: "office relocation", "new CRM system", "team restructure"
- The "What Is NOT Changing" slide is crucial — it reduces anxiety
- Add "Include a slide for managers to use with their teams" for cascade comms
- Follow up with "Draft a companion email announcement for all staff"
