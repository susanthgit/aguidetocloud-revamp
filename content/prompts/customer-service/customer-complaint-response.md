## Tips for Best Results

- Paste the actual customer complaint below the prompt for a tailored response
- In **Outlook**, select the complaint email and ask Copilot to draft a reply
- The key differentiator is specificity — generic apologies feel robotic
- Follow up with "Create an internal case note documenting this complaint and resolution"
