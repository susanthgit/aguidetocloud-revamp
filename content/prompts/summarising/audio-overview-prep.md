## Tips for Best Results

- **NotebookLM Audio Overview** creates a ~10 minute AI podcast from your sources
- Upload 3-8 diverse sources for the richest discussion
- Add a "briefing note" document to steer the conversation
- Great for: study revision, onboarding material, content research
- The output is shareable — useful for team learning
