## Tips for Best Results

- Use this in **Copilot Chat (Work mode)** — it reads your actual Outlook inbox
- Run it every Monday morning to plan your week
- Follow up with "Draft a response to the urgent ones" for a productivity chain
- Add "from the last 3 days" if a full week is too much
