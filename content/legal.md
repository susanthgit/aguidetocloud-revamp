**Last updated:** 18 April 2026

This privacy policy explains what data **A Guide to Cloud & AI** (aguidetocloud.com) collects, why, who receives it, and your rights. This is a personal educational website run by Susanth Sutheesh. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Microsoft.

## What We Collect

### Analytics (with your consent)

We use **Google Analytics 4** and **Microsoft Clarity**. **Neither activates until you click Accept on the cookie banner.** No personal information (name, email, IP) is collected. Clarity does not capture keystrokes.

### Feedback Form

Submissions (category, subject, message, optional name) go to public [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/susanthgit/aguidetocloud-feedback/discussions). **Email addresses are never published.** Do not include sensitive information.

### Browser Storage

Tools use `localStorage` for preferences (favourites, progress). Data **never leaves your browser**.

### Password Breach Check

The optional breach check sends only the first 5 characters of a SHA-1 hash to [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) (k-anonymity). Your actual password never leaves your browser.

## Cookies

- **Analytics:** `_ga`, `_clck` etc. (GA4 + Clarity) -- **only set after consent**
- **Functional:** `agtc_consent` -- remembers your consent choice
- **Necessary:** `__cf_bm` -- Cloudflare bot protection

## Third-Party Services

Google Analytics ([privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)), Microsoft Clarity ([terms](https://clarity.microsoft.com/terms)), Cloudflare ([privacy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)), GitHub ([privacy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)), Have I Been Pwned ([privacy](https://haveibeenpwned.com/Privacy)), YouTube ([privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)). No data is sold or shared for advertising.

## Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

- **Access, correct, or delete** your personal information
- **Withdraw consent** -- clear `agtc_consent` from localStorage and refresh
- **Opt out** -- click Reject on the banner, or use an ad blocker, or [Google Opt-Out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## Data Retention

Google Analytics: 14 months. Clarity: 30 days. Feedback: indefinite (public). localStorage: until you clear it.

## Children

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the [feedback form](/feedback/) or [about page](/about/).
