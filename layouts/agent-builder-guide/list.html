The #1 Question: What happens when I share my agent?

Short answer: each user sees their own data. Your emails, Teams chats, and files are never exposed to other users. The agent respects existing M365 permissions.

Exception: Embedded (uploaded) files ARE shared with everyone who has agent access. Use SharePoint for sensitive documents.
