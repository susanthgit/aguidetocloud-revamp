Hi, I'm Susanth Sutheesh — a Sr AI Solution Engineer at Microsoft New Zealand by day, and an introvert who'd rather sit in the shadow than the spotlight by night. I work in cloud and AI for a living. In my head, I'm still that curious intern — figuring it out for myself first, simplifying it until it actually clicks. What overflows lands here.

I still get butterflies before every customer meeting. After 18+ years, every new product, every new conversation still feels like learning from scratch. That's what keeps me sharing — if something took me a while to understand, someone else probably needs the simple version too.

Why I Started This

I started A Guide to Cloud & AI in 2020 as a personal project — a way to keep the things I learned from getting lost. Coming from a non-English-speaking background, I know how hard tech feels when the docs are dense and the paid courses cost more than your rent.

That's why I create beginner-friendly tutorials, hands-on labs, study guides, free tools, and job interview prep videos — all focused on real-world learning.

Every Copilot quirk, every Azure service that finally clicks, every licensing diagram I draw for myself — it ends up here. Free where I can keep it free. For people like us — IT pros, students, and anyone who's tired of marketing-speak.

It's not strategy. It's overflow.

The AI Pivot

Most of my content today is AI-first — Microsoft 365 Copilot, GitHub Copilot, Copilot Studio, MCP servers, AI agents, Azure AI Foundry. That's where the industry is heading, and frankly that's where I'm spending most of my own learning hours. I'd rather share what I'm figuring out in real time than wait until I've "mastered" it — mastery is a moving target anyway.

The future is AI, and I want to help you navigate it.

Beyond Technology

When I step away from screens, I ground myself through yoga, meditation, and the study of ancient spiritual texts. As a certified yoga instructor, I spend a lot of time contemplating life and the deeper spiritual realm — it keeps me steady while the tech world keeps moving the goalposts.

Most importantly, I cherish time with my wife and daughter — my greatest source of inspiration and joy. Everything else is downstream of that.
