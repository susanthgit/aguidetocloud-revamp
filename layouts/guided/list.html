What is IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS?

⏱️ 8 min 🟢 Beginner Module 3 of 10

☕ The Simple Version

Imagine you need pizza tonight...

🏠 Made at home (IaaS) — You buy ingredients, make the dough, bake it yourself. Maximum control, maximum effort.

🍕 Take & bake (PaaS) — You buy a pre-made pizza, put it in YOUR oven. They did the hard part, you just cook it.

🛵 Delivered (SaaS) — It shows up at your door, ready to eat. You just enjoy it.

🃏 Flashcards (5)

IaaS
Infrastructure as a Service — you rent VMs, storage & networking. You manage the OS and apps.
PaaS
Platform as a Service — you deploy your code, the provider manages the infrastructure underneath.
SaaS
Software as a Service — fully managed apps you access via the web. E.g., Microsoft 365, Gmail.
