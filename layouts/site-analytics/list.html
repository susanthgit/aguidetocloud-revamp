Launching Soon

A fully transparent analytics dashboard — built for you, not advertisers.

We believe in radical transparency. While most websites keep their numbers locked behind closed doors, we're building something different — a public, real-time analytics dashboard that shows exactly how this site and its 18 free tools are being used.

No vanity metrics. No inflated numbers. Just honest data — how many people visit, which tools they use most, what they search for, and where they come from. All collected without cookies or personal data.

This dashboard will combine data from Google Analytics (traffic patterns), Google Search Console (how people find us), and our own anonymous event tracking (which tools get the most use) — all served through a public API at /api/stats that anyone can query.

What people actually search for on Google to find this site — real queries, click rates, and positions.

🔒

100% Anonymous

No cookies, no personal data, no tracking pixels. Just aggregate counts — because transparency shouldn't cost your privacy.

Why open analytics?

Most websites hide their numbers. We think that's backwards. If you're using our tools, you deserve to see how they're doing. Every number on this dashboard will be real, unfiltered, and open to everyone.

We're collecting data now and building the dashboard. Once we have enough meaningful data to show, this page will go live with interactive charts, leaderboards, and insights.

🛠️ Building in progress — check back in a few weeks, or follow us for the launch announcement.
