What the Numbers Mean

WoW — Week-over-Week. Compares this week to last week.

Health Score — 0-100 composite: growth (25) + SEO (25) + engagement (25) + reach (25).

Velocity — Views per day since publish. Shows what's growing NOW, not just old hits.

Engagement — (Likes + Comments) / Views. How much your audience interacts.

Title Score — 0-100 rating: power words, length, specificity, curiosity gap.

Quick Wins — SEO queries at position 8-20. One content push could reach page 1.

Content Bets — Suggested next content based on search demand + what's working.

Topic Clusters — All content grouped by theme. Shows which topics drive growth.

Portfolio Score — Health of your entire content portfolio: blogs + tools + videos + SEO.

Data Sources

GA4 — Site traffic, page views, users, countries, devices. Refreshes every 5 min.

GSC — Google Search Console. Queries, positions, CTR. Lags ~2 days.

YouTube — Channel stats + video data. Refreshes every 6 hours.

Realtime — Live visitor count. Updates every 30 seconds.
