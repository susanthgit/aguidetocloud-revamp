The CRAFTS Framework

Every great AI prompt includes six elements. The Prompt Polisher scores yours against each one.
Need ready-made prompts? Browse our AI Prompt Library →

C Context

Background, situation, or domain that helps the AI understand your world

R Role

The persona or expertise you want the AI to adopt

A Action

A clear, specific task with strong verbs — the core instruction

F Format

How you want the output — bullets, table, code, paragraphs, etc.

T Tone

The audience and voice — professional, casual, technical, beginner-friendly

S Scope

Constraints — length, focus areas, what to include or exclude
