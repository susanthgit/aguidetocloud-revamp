Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who reads my feedback?

Susanth (the site creator) personally reads every submission. Feedback is posted publicly as a GitHub Discussion so the community can upvote and comment.

2. Do I need to sign up or log in?

No. You can submit feedback completely anonymously — name and email are optional. No account required.

3. What kind of feedback can I submit?

Anything! Feature requests, bug reports, video requests, content ideas, tool suggestions, or general questions about Microsoft 365, Azure, and AI.

4. Will I get a reply?

If you provide your email, you'll be notified when there's a response on your discussion. Otherwise, check back on this page — all discussions are public.

5. How are feature requests prioritised?

Community upvotes matter! Popular requests get built first. Items marked "Planned" or "In Progress" are actively being worked on.

6. Is my data private?

Your feedback text is posted publicly on GitHub Discussions. Name and email are optional — if you want to stay anonymous, just leave them blank.
